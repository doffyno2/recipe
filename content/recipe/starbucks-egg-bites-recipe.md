<p>Are you a fan of Starbucks egg bites? You're not alone! These delicious and portable breakfast bites have become a popular choice for busy mornings. But did you know that you can easily make them at home? In this article, we'll show you how to make Starbucks egg bites recipe that will rival the original recipe. Let's get started!</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Starbucks Egg Bites Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Starbucks%20Egg%20Bites%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Starbucks Egg Bites Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before we get started, let's gather all the ingredients we need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>6 eggs</li>
  <li>1/2 cup cottage cheese</li>
  <li>1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese</li>
  <li>1/4 cup chopped cooked bacon</li>
  <li>1/4 cup chopped roasted red peppers</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon pepper</li>
  <li>Non-stick cooking spray</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Starbucks Egg Bites Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=For%20The%20Marinade%20Starbucks%20Egg%20Bites%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="For The Marinade Starbucks Egg Bites Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>To make the egg bites extra flavorful, we'll make a quick marinade:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1/4 cup whole milk</li>
  <li>1/4 cup sour cream</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon hot sauce</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Starbucks Egg Bites Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Starbucks%20Egg%20Bites%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Starbucks Egg Bites Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that we have everything we need, let's make the egg bites:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat your oven to 300°F.</li>
  <li>In a blender or food processor, blend together the eggs, cottage cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, bacon, roasted red peppers, salt, and pepper until smooth.</li>
  <li>In a separate mixing bowl, whisk together the milk, sour cream, and hot sauce.</li>
  <li>Add the egg mixture to the bowl and whisk everything together until well combined.</li>
  <li>Spray a muffin tin with non-stick cooking spray.</li>
  <li>Pour the egg mixture into the muffin cups, filling each one about 3/4 full.</li>
  <li>Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the egg bites are set and lightly golden on top.</li>
  <li>Let the egg bites cool for a few minutes before removing them from the muffin tin.</li>
  <li>Serve and enjoy!</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Starbucks Egg Bites Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Cook%20Starbucks%20Egg%20Bites%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Cook Starbucks Egg Bites Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>You can cook the egg bites in the oven as described above, or you can use a sous vide machine if you have one. To cook the egg bites sous vide:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Place the egg mixture in a large plastic bag and remove as much air as possible.</li>
  <li>Set your sous vide machine to 172°F.</li>
  <li>Place the bag in the sous vide machine and cook for 1 hour.</li>
  <li>Remove the bag from the sous vide machine and let it cool for a few minutes before opening it.</li>
  <li>Remove the egg bites from the bag and serve.</li>
</ol>
