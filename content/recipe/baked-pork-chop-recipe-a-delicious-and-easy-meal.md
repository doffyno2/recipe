<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Baked Pork Chop Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20For%20Baked%20Pork%20Chop%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients For Baked Pork Chop Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>For this recipe, you will need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
<li>4 bone-in pork chops</li>
<li>2 tablespoons olive oil</li>
<li>2 tablespoons soy sauce</li>
<li>1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce</li>
<li>1 tablespoon honey</li>
<li>1 teaspoon garlic powder</li>
<li>1 teaspoon onion powder</li>
<li>1/2 teaspoon paprika</li>
<li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Baked Pork Chop Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Marinade%20For%20Baked%20Pork%20Chop%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Marinade For Baked Pork Chop Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before cooking the pork chops, you’ll want to marinate them for at least 30 minutes. Here’s how:</p>

<ol>
<li>In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, honey, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.</li>
<li>Place pork chops in a large ziplock bag and pour marinade over the top. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 24 hours).</li>
</ol>

<h2>Kitchen Tools Needed for Baked Pork Chop Recipe</h2>
<center>

<p>You don’t need any fancy kitchen tools for this recipe. Here’s what you’ll need:</p>

<ul>
<li>Large ziplock bag</li>
<li>Baking dish</li>
<li>Foil</li>
<li>Measuring spoons</li>
<li>Whisk</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Baked Pork Chop Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Baked%20Pork%20Chop%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Baked Pork Chop Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now it’s time to cook the pork chops. Follow these steps:</p>

<ol>
<li>Preheat oven to 375°F.</li>
<li>Remove pork chops from marinade and place in a single layer in a baking dish.</li>
<li>Drizzle remaining marinade over the top of the pork chops.</li>
<li>Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.</li>
<li>Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the pork chops reaches 145°F.</li>
<li>Let the pork chops rest for 3-5 minutes before serving.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Baked Pork Chop Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Cook%20Baked%20Pork%20Chop%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Cook Baked Pork Chop Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you know how to make the perfect baked pork chop, let’s talk about how to cook it. Here are some tips:</p>

<ul>
<li>Make sure to marinate the pork chops for at least 30 minutes. This will add flavor and help tenderize the meat.</li>
<li>Don’t overcook the pork chops. Use a meat thermometer to make sure the internal temperature reaches 145°F.</li>
<li>Let the pork chops rest for a few minutes before serving. This will allow the juices to redistribute and make the meat more tender.</li>
<li>Pair the pork chops with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes.</li>
</ul>
