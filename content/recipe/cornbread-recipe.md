<p>Cornbread is a classic Southern dish that is loved by many. It is a delicious and easy-to-make bread that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make the perfect cornbread recipe. We will cover everything from the ingredients needed, the marination process, the kitchen tools required, to how to cook this mouth-watering dish.</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Cornbread Recipe</h2>

<p>Before we dive into the recipe, let us first look at the ingredients that are required to make cornbread. You will need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 cup of cornmeal</li>
  <li>1 cup of all-purpose flour</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of baking powder</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>2 eggs</li>
  <li>1 cup of milk</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of vegetable oil or melted butter</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of honey (optional)</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Cornbread Recipe</h2>

<p>To make the best cornbread, it is important to marinate it properly. Here is how you can do it:</p>

<ul>
  <li>In a large bowl, mix the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt together.</li>
  <li>In another bowl, beat the eggs and add milk, vegetable oil or melted butter, and honey (if using).</li>
  <li>Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.</li>
  <li>Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 2 hours).</li>
</ul>

<h2>Kitchen Tools Needed for Cornbread Recipe</h2>

<p>Before you start cooking the cornbread, you will need the following kitchen tools:</p>

<ul>
  <li>A large mixing bowl</li>
  <li>A whisk or a fork</li>
  <li>A 9-inch cast-iron skillet or a baking dish</li>
  <li>An oven</li>
  <li>A spatula</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Cornbread Recipe</h2>

<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and the necessary kitchen tools, it's time to start making the cornbread. Here is how you can do it:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat the oven to 400Â°F.</li>
  <li>Grease the cast-iron skillet or baking dish with butter or vegetable oil.</li>
  <li>Take out the cornbread batter from the refrigerator and stir it gently.</li>
  <li>Pour the batter into the prepared skillet or baking dish.</li>
  <li>Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cornbread is golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.</li>
  <li>Remove the skillet or baking dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.</li>
  <li>Serve the cornbread warm with butter, honey, or your favorite topping.</li>
</ol>
