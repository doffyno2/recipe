<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Banana Cream Pie Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Banana%20Cream%20Pie%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Banana Cream Pie Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Making a mouth-watering banana cream pie is easier than you might think. You only need a few ingredients, some of which you probably already have in your pantry. Here's what you need to make the perfect banana cream pie:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 pre-baked 9-inch pie crust</li>
  <li>3 ripe bananas, sliced</li>
  <li>1/2 cup sugar</li>
  <li>1/3 cup cornstarch</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon salt</li>
  <li>2 cups whole milk</li>
  <li>3 egg yolks</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon unsalted butter</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
  <li>1 cup heavy cream</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons powdered sugar</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Banana Cream Pie Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Banana%20Cream%20Pie%20Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Banana Cream Pie Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">

<p>The bananas are the star of the show when it comes to banana cream pie, so make sure they're perfectly ripe. Slice them into rounds, and set them aside while you prepare the rest of the filling.</p>

<center>

  <li>Whisk</li>
  <li>Wooden spoon</li>

</ul>

<h2>How to Make Banana Cream Pie Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Banana%20Cream%20Pie&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Banana Cream Pie" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have all your ingredients and tools ready, let's get started on making your delicious banana cream pie.</p>

<ol>
  <li>In a medium saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and salt.</li>
  <li>Add the milk and whisk until smooth.</li>
  <li>Place the saucepan over medium heat and stir constantly with a wooden spoon until the mixture comes to a boil and thickens, about 10 minutes.</li>
  <li>Remove from heat and whisk in the egg yolks, one at a time.</li>
  <li>Add the butter and vanilla extract and whisk until smooth.</li>
  <li>Place the sliced bananas in the bottom of the pre-baked pie crust.</li>
  <li>Pour the custard mixture over the bananas, spreading it evenly.</li>
  <li>Cover the pie with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until set.</li>
  <li>In a large mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream and powdered sugar with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.</li>
  <li>Transfer the whipped cream to a piping bag and pipe it over the top of the pie.</li>
  <li>Refrigerate the pie until ready to serve.</li>
</ol>
