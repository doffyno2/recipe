<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Egg Bake Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20For%20Egg%20Bake%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients For Egg Bake Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before we get into the recipe, let's go over the ingredients you'll need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>10 eggs</li>
  <li>1/2 cup milk</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon black pepper</li>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped onion</li>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped bell pepper</li>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped mushrooms</li>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped spinach</li>
  <li>1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese</li>
  <li>1/4 cup cooked and crumbled bacon (optional)</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Marinade%20For%20Egg%20Bake%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Marinade For Egg Bake Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have your ingredients, let's make the marinade:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>Add the milk, salt, and pepper and whisk until well combined.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Make Egg Bake Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Egg%20Bake%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Egg Bake Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have everything you need, let's make the egg bake:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).</li>
  <li>Grease your baking dish with cooking spray or butter.</li>
  <li>Sauté the onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, and spinach in a frying pan until they are soft.</li>
  <li>Pour the vegetables into the greased baking dish.</li>
  <li>Pour the marinade over the vegetables.</li>
  <li>Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the top.</li>
  <li>Add the cooked and crumbled bacon on top (if using).</li>
  <li>Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes, or until the egg bake is set and the cheese is melted and bubbly.</li>
  <li>Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.</li>
</ol>
