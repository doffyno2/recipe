<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Waffle Batter Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Waffle%20Batter%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Waffle Batter Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Before we dive into the recipe, let's take a look at the ingredients needed to make waffle batter:</p>
<ul>
  <li>2 cups all-purpose flour</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon baking powder</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon salt</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons granulated sugar</li>
  <li>1 3/4 cups milk</li>
  <li>1/3 cup vegetable oil</li>
  <li>2 large eggs</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Waffle Batter Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Waffle%20Batter%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Waffle Batter Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and kitchen tools ready, let's start making waffle batter:</p>
<ol>
  <li>In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.</li>
  <li>In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract (if using).</li>
  <li>Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. Do not overmix, as this can result in tough waffles.</li>
  <li>Preheat your waffle maker according to the manufacturer's instructions. Once heated, spray the waffle maker with nonstick cooking spray.</li>
  <li>Pour the waffle batter onto the center of the waffle maker, using a ladle or measuring cup. The amount of batter needed will depend on the size of your waffle maker.</li>
  <li>Cook the waffles according to the manufacturer's instructions, until golden brown and crispy.</li>
  <li>Repeat with the remaining batter, spraying the waffle maker with cooking spray between each batch.</li>
  <li>Serve the waffles immediately, topped with your favorite toppings such as butter, syrup, whipped cream, or fresh fruit.</li>
</ol>
