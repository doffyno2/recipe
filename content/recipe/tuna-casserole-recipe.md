<p>Before we start cooking, let's gather all of the necessary ingredients. Here's what you'll need:</p>

<ul>

  <li>1 cup of macaroni</li>
  <li>1 can of cream of mushroom soup</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of milk</li>

  <li>1/4 cup of chopped celery</li>

<h2>How to Make Tuna Casserole Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Tuna%20Casserole%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Tuna Casserole Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that we have all of our ingredients and kitchen tools ready, let's start making the tuna casserole. Follow these easy steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat the oven to 375°F.</li>
  <li>Cook the macaroni according to the instructions on the package. Drain and set aside.</li>
  <li>In a frying pan, cook the onion and celery until they are soft.</li>
  <li>Add the can of cream of mushroom soup, milk, shredded cheddar cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until the cheese is melted and the ingredients are well combined.</li>
  <li>Add the tuna and cooked macaroni to the pan. Mix well.</li>
  <li>Grease a baking dish with butter and add the tuna mixture.</li>
  <li>Sprinkle bread crumbs on top of the mixture.</li>
  <li>Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the bread crumbs are golden brown.</li>
  <li>Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.</li>
</ol>
