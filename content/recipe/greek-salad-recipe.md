<p>A Greek salad is a refreshing and healthy dish that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you are looking for a light lunch or a side dish to serve at your next dinner party, this Greek salad recipe is sure to impress. In this article, we will guide you through the ingredients needed, the marinade, kitchen tools, and the steps to make and cook a delicious Greek salad. Let's get started!</p>

<h2>Ingredients needed to make Greek Salad Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Greek%20Salad&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Greek Salad" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Here are the ingredients you will need to make a traditional Greek salad:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 large cucumber, chopped</li>
  <li>3-4 ripe tomatoes, chopped</li>
  <li>1 small red onion, sliced</li>
  <li>1 green bell pepper, sliced</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of Kalamata olives, pitted</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of Feta cheese, crumbled</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of extra-virgin olive oil</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of red wine vinegar</li>
  <li>1 tsp of dried oregano</li>
  <li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Greek Salad Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=For%20The%20Marinade%20Greek%20Salad%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="For The Marinade Greek Salad Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>The marinade is what gives the Greek salad its delicious flavor. Here is how to make it:</p>

<ol>
  <li>In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.</li>
  <li>Add the sliced red onions to the marinade and let them sit for at least 15 minutes. This will help to mellow out the flavor of the onions.</li>
  <li>Once the onions have marinated, remove them from the marinade and set them aside.</li>
  <li>Pour the remaining marinade over the vegetables and toss to coat.</li>
  <li>Add the crumbled Feta cheese and Kalamata olives on top and serve.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Make Greek Salad Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Greek%20Salad%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Greek Salad Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and kitchen tools, let's get started with the steps to make this delicious Greek salad:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Chop the cucumber, tomatoes, and green bell pepper into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>Slice the red onion and add it to the mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>Add the Kalamata olives to the mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper to make the marinade.</li>
  <li>Add the sliced red onions to the marinade and let them sit for at least 15 minutes.</li>
  <li>Once the onions have marinated, remove them from the marinade and set them aside.</li>
  <li>Pour the remaining marinade over the vegetables and toss to coat.</li>
  <li>Add the crumbled Feta cheese and Kalamata olives on top and serve.</li>
</ol>
