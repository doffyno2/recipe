<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Blueberry Muffin Recipe</h2>

<p>To make these delicious blueberry muffins, you'll need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
  <li>2 cups all-purpose flour</li>
  <li>2 tsp baking powder</li>
  <li>1/4 tsp salt</li>
  <li>1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened</li>
  <li>3/4 cup granulated sugar</li>
  <li>2 large eggs</li>
  <li>1 tsp vanilla extract</li>
  <li>1/2 cup milk</li>
  <li>1 1/2 cups fresh blueberries</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Blueberry Muffin Recipe</h2>

<p>Now that you have all your ingredients and kitchen tools ready, it's time to get started on making these delicious blueberry muffins. Here's a step-by-step guide:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line your muffin tin with paper muffin cups.</li>
  <li>In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt until well combined.</li>
  <li>In a separate bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.</li>
  <li>Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the butter mixture and beat until well combined.</li>
  <li>Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with the milk, and mix until just combined.</li>
  <li>Gently fold in the blueberries with a spatula, being careful not to overmix the batter.</li>
  <li>Scoop the batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each one about 2/3 full.</li>
  <li>Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until the muffins are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.</li>
  <li>Remove from the oven and allow the muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.</li>
</ol>
