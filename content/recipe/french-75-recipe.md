<p>French 75 is a classic cocktail that has been around for more than a century. It is believed to have been named after the French 75mm field gun used during World War I because of its explosive kick. This cocktail is made with gin, lemon juice, sugar, and topped up with champagne. In this article, we will go through the ingredients needed to make French 75, the marinating process, kitchen tools needed, and how to make and cook French 75. We will also answer some frequently asked questions about this cocktail.</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make French 75 Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20For%20French%2075%20Cocktail&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients For French 75 Cocktail" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>The following ingredients are needed to make French 75:</p>
<ul>
    <li>2 oz. gin</li>
    <li>1 oz. fresh lemon juice</li>
    <li>1/2 oz. simple syrup</li>
    <li>2 oz. champagne or sparkling wine</li>
    <li>Ice</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make French 75 Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20French%2075%20Cocktail&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make French 75 Cocktail" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Follow these steps to make French 75:</p>
<ol>
    <li>Fill the cocktail shaker with ice.</li>
    <li>Add gin, fresh lemon juice, and simple syrup to the shaker.</li>
    <li>Shake the ingredients well for about 10-15 seconds.</li>
    <li>Strain the mixture into a champagne flute.</li>
    <li>Top up the glass with champagne or sparkling wine.</li>
    <li>Garnish with a lemon twist or a cherry.</li>
</ol>
