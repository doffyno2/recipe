<p>Fruit pizza is a delicious and colorful dessert that is perfect for any occasion. It is a combination of a crispy cookie crust, creamy frosting, and fresh fruits. This recipe is easy to make and requires only a few ingredients. In this article, we will show you how to make a fruit pizza step by step. </p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Fruit Pizza Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Fruit%20Pizza%20Recipe%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Fruit Pizza Recipe Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>The following are the ingredients that you will need to make a fruit pizza:</p>
<ul>
    <li>1 package of refrigerated sugar cookie dough</li>
    <li>8 oz. cream cheese, softened</li>
    <li>1/4 cup granulated sugar</li>
    <li>1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
    <li>Assorted fresh fruits (such as strawberries, kiwi, blueberries, and grapes)</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Fruit Pizza Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Fruit%20Pizza%20Recipe%20Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Fruit Pizza Recipe Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before assembling the fruit pizza, you will need to make a marinade for the fruits. This will help to enhance the flavor of the fruits and make them more tender. Here's how to make the marinade:</p>
<ul>
    <li>1/4 cup orange juice</li>
    <li>2 tablespoons honey</li>
    <li>1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
    <li>1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon</li>
</ul>
<p>Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl and set aside.</p>

<h2>How to Make Fruit Pizza Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Fruit%20Pizza%20Recipe%20Making&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Fruit Pizza Recipe Making" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Follow these simple steps to make a fruit pizza:</p>
<ol>
    <li>Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.</li>
    <li>Grease a 9-inch round baking pan with cooking spray.</li>
    <li>Roll out the sugar cookie dough and press it into the prepared baking pan.</li>
    <li>Bake the cookie crust for 12-15 minutes or until lightly golden brown.</li>
    <li>Remove the crust from the oven and let it cool completely.</li>
    <li>In a mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.</li>
    <li>Spread the cream cheese mixture over the cooled cookie crust.</li>
    <li>Arrange the sliced fruits on top of the cream cheese mixture.</li>
    <li>Drizzle the fruit marinade over the fruits.</li>
    <li>Refrigerate the fruit pizza for at least 1 hour before serving.</li>
</ol>
