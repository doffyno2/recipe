<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Chicken%20Noodle%20Soup%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Here are the ingredients you'll need to make chicken noodle soup:</p>

<ul>
<li>1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces</li>
<li>8 cups chicken broth</li>
<li>1 cup chopped onion</li>
<li>1 cup chopped carrots</li>
<li>1 cup chopped celery</li>
<li>3 cloves garlic, minced</li>
<li>2 bay leaves</li>
<li>1 teaspoon dried thyme</li>
<li>1 teaspoon dried rosemary</li>
<li>8 ounces egg noodles</li>
<li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Chicken%20Noodle%20Soup%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Here's how to make chicken noodle soup:</p>

<ol>
<li>In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium heat.</li>
<li>Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.</li>
<li>Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.</li>
<li>Add the chicken broth, bay leaves, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Bring the soup to a boil.</li>
<li>Reduce the heat to low and add the chicken. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.</li>
<li>Add the egg noodles and cook for about 8-10 minutes or until the noodles are tender.</li>
<li>Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.</li>
<li>Remove the bay leaves and serve the soup hot.</li>
</ol>
