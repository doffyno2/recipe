<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Tuna%20Noodle%20Casserole%20Recipe%20Ingredients%20Image&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe Ingredients Image" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before we dive into the cooking process, let's gather all the ingredients you'll need to make this tuna noodle casserole:</p>

<ul>
    <li>1 pound egg noodles</li>
    <li>2 cans of tuna, drained</li>
    <li>1 cup frozen peas</li>
    <li>1 cup sliced mushrooms</li>
    <li>1/2 cup chopped onion</li>
    <li>1/2 cup chopped celery</li>
    <li>2 cloves garlic, minced</li>
    <li>2 cups milk</li>
    <li>1/4 cup all-purpose flour</li>
    <li>1/4 cup butter</li>
    <li>1/2 teaspoon salt</li>
    <li>1/4 teaspoon black pepper</li>
    <li>1 cup shredded cheddar cheese</li>
    <li>1/2 cup breadcrumbs</li>
</ul>

<p>Now that you have all the ingredients, it's time to start cooking!</p>

<h2>How to Make Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Tuna%20Noodle%20Casserole%20Recipe%20How%20To%20Make%20Image&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe How To Make Image" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Follow these steps to make this delicious tuna noodle casserole:</p>

<ol>
    <li>Preheat the oven to 375°F.</li>
    <li>Cook the egg noodles according to package directions, then drain and set aside.</li>
    <li>In a large skillet, sauté the onion, celery, mushrooms, and garlic over medium heat until softened, about 5 minutes.</li>
    <li>Add the tuna and frozen peas to the skillet, and cook for another 2-3 minutes.</li>
    <li>In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour, salt, and pepper until smooth.</li>
    <li>Add the milk to the saucepan, and whisk until the sauce thickens and bubbles, about 5 minutes.</li>
    <li>Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.</li>
    <li>Add the cooked noodles to the skillet with the tuna and vegetables, and mix well.</li>
    <li>Pour the cheese sauce over the noodle mixture, and stir until well combined.</li>
    <li>Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish, and sprinkle the breadcrumbs over the top.</li>
    <li>Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the casserole is hot and bubbly.</li>
    <li>Let cool for a few minutes before serving.</li>
</ol>
