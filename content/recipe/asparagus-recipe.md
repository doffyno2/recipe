<h2>How to Make Asparagus Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Asparagus%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Asparagus Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Follow these simple steps to make the perfect asparagus recipe:</p>
<ol>
<li>Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).</li>
<li>Wash the asparagus and trim off the woody ends.</li>
<li>Blanch the asparagus in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then immediately transfer them to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process.</li>
<li>Pat the asparagus dry with a paper towel and place them on a baking sheet.</li>
<li>Brush the marinade over the asparagus and season with salt and pepper.</li>
<li>Bake the asparagus for 10-12 minutes, until they are tender and slightly charred.</li>
<li>Remove the asparagus from the oven and serve immediately.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Asparagus Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Cook%20Asparagus%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Cook Asparagus Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">

<p>Cooking asparagus is easy and straightforward. Here are some simple steps to follow:</p>
<ol>
<li>Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).</li>
<li>Wash the asparagus and trim off the woody ends.</li>
<li>Place the asparagus on a baking sheet and coat them with olive oil.</li>
<li>Season the asparagus with salt and pepper.</li>
<li>Bake the asparagus for 10-12 minutes, until they are tender and slightly charred.</li>
<li>Remove the asparagus from the oven and serve immediately.</li>
</ol>
