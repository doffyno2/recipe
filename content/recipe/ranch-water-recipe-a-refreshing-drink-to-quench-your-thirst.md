<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Ranch Water Recipe</h2>

<center>

<p>To make ranch water, you'll need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
	<li>2 oz of tequila</li>
	<li>1 oz of lime juice</li>
	<li>4-6 oz of Topo Chico</li>
</ul>

<p>These ingredients can be adjusted to your liking, depending on how strong you want your drink.</p>

<h2>Marinade Recipe for Ranch Water</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ranch%20Water%20Recipe%20Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ranch Water Recipe Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>If you want to add a little extra flavor to your ranch water, you can make a marinade using the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
	<li>1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro</li>
	<li>1/4 cup of chopped fresh mint</li>
	<li>1 tablespoon of honey</li>
	<li>1/2 teaspoon of salt</li>
	<li>1/2 teaspoon of cumin</li>
	<li>1/2 teaspoon of chili powder</li>
	<li>1/4 cup of lime juice</li>
</ul>

<p>To make the marinade, simply combine all of the ingredients in a bowl and whisk together until well combined. Let the mixture sit for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. When ready to use, strain the marinade through a fine-mesh sieve and discard the solids.</p>

<h2>How to Make Ranch Water Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Ranch%20Water%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Ranch Water Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have your ingredients and kitchen tools ready, here's how to make ranch water:</p>

<ol>
	<li>Fill your cocktail shaker with ice.</li>
	<li>Add 2 oz of tequila and 1 oz of lime juice to the shaker.</li>
	<li>Shake well for 10-15 seconds.</li>
	<li>Strain the mixture into a highball glass filled with ice.</li>
	<li>Top the glass off with 4-6 oz of Topo Chico.</li>
	<li>Garnish with a lime wedge or a sprig of fresh mint.</li>
	<li>Enjoy!</li>
</ol>
