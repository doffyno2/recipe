<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Corn Tortilla Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Corn%20Tortilla%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Corn Tortilla Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Here are the ingredients you will need to make corn tortillas:</p>

<ul>
  <li>2 cups masa harina</li>
  <li>1 1/2 cups warm water</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon salt</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Corn Tortilla Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Corn%20Tortilla%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Corn Tortilla Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Follow these simple steps to make corn tortillas:</p>

<ol>
  <li>In a mixing bowl, combine the masa harina and salt.</li>
  <li>Add the warm water to the bowl and stir until a dough forms.</li>
  <li>Knead the dough for a few minutes until it becomes smooth and pliable.</li>
  <li>Divide the dough into small balls, about the size of a golf ball.</li>
  <li>Place a ball of dough on a piece of parchment paper or plastic wrap.</li>
  <li>Place another piece of parchment paper or plastic wrap on top of the dough ball.</li>
  <li>Use a tortilla press or heavy skillet to flatten the dough ball into a thin round tortilla.</li>
  <li>Carefully peel the parchment paper or plastic wrap off the tortilla.</li>
  <li>Repeat with the remaining dough balls.</li>
  <li>Cook the tortillas in a dry skillet or on a griddle over medium-high heat for about 30 seconds on each side, or until they are lightly browned and slightly puffed.</li>
  <li>Keep the tortillas warm in a clean kitchen towel until ready to serve.</li>
</ol>
