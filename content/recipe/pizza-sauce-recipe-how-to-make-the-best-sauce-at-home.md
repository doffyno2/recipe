<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Pizza Sauce Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Pizza%20Sauce%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Pizza Sauce Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>The first step in making the perfect pizza sauce is gathering all of the necessary ingredients. Here are the ingredients you'll need to make this pizza sauce recipe:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of olive oil</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of dried basil</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of dried oregano</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of garlic powder</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of onion powder</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Pizza Sauce Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Pizza%20Sauce%20Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Pizza Sauce Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Marinading the sauce is an optional step, but it can add extra flavor to your pizza. Here's how to make the marinade:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 tablespoon of olive oil</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of honey</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of dried basil</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of dried oregano</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of garlic powder</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of onion powder</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)</li>
</ul>

<p>Mix the marinade ingredients together in a bowl and pour it over the crushed tomatoes. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes before using.</p>

<h2>How to Make Pizza Sauce Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Pizza%20Sauce%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Pizza Sauce Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have all of your ingredients and kitchen tools ready, it's time to make the pizza sauce. Here are the step-by-step instructions:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Open the can of crushed tomatoes and pour it into a mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>Add the olive oil, dried basil, dried oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using) to the bowl.</li>
  <li>Whisk the ingredients together until they are well combined.</li>
  <li>If using the marinade, pour it over the sauce and mix well.</li>
  <li>Let the sauce sit for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.</li>
  <li>Use the sauce on your favorite pizza recipe and enjoy!</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Pizza Sauce Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Cook%20Pizza%20Sauce%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Cook Pizza Sauce Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">

<p>When it comes to cooking pizza sauce, it's important to remember that the sauce should be heated before adding it to the pizza. Here's how to cook the pizza sauce:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Add the sauce to a small saucepan.</li>
  <li>Heat the sauce over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it is heated through.</li>
  <li>Use the heated sauce on your favorite pizza recipe.</li>
</ol>
