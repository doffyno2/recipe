<h2>Introduction</h2>

Spring rolls, a popular appetizer in Asian cuisine, are easy to make and perfect for any occasion. The crispy texture, combined with the savory filling, makes for a delicious and satisfying snack. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide to making spring rolls, including the ingredients, marinade, kitchen tools, cooking methods, and frequently asked questions.

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Spring Roll Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Spring%20Roll%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Spring Roll Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

To make spring rolls, you will need the following ingredients:

<ul>
  <li>Spring roll wrappers</li>
  <li>1 pound of ground pork</li>
  <li>1 cup of shredded carrots</li>
  <li>1 cup of chopped cabbage</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of chopped onion</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of minced garlic</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of soy sauce</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of fish sauce</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of sugar</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of vegetable oil</li>
  <li>1 egg, beaten</li>
</ul>

<h2>Marinade for Spring Roll Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Marinade%20For%20Spring%20Roll%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Marinade For Spring Roll Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">

To make the marinade for the spring rolls, you will need:

<ul>
  <li>1 tablespoon of soy sauce</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of fish sauce</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of sugar</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
</ul>

Mix all the ingredients together in a small bowl and set it aside.

<h2>How to Make Spring Roll Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Spring%20Roll%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Spring Roll Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

Follow these steps to make spring rolls:

<ol>
  <li>In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, shredded carrots, chopped cabbage, chopped onion, minced garlic, soy sauce, fish sauce, sugar, and black pepper. Mix well.</li>
  <li>Place a spring roll wrapper on a flat surface and brush the edges with beaten egg.</li>
  <li>Add 2 tablespoons of the pork mixture to the center of the wrapper.</li>
  <li>Fold the bottom edge of the wrapper over the filling and tuck it under.</li>
  <li>Roll the wrapper halfway up and fold the sides in.</li>
  <li>Continue rolling until the spring roll is completely sealed.</li>
  <li>Repeat the process with the remaining wrappers and filling.</li>
  <li>Preheat the oven to 375°F.</li>
  <li>Place the spring rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.</li>
  <li>Brush the spring rolls with vegetable oil.</li>
  <li>Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.</li>
  <li>Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.</li>
</ol>

To cook spring rolls, follow these steps:

<ol>
  <li>Preheat the oven to 375°F.</li>
  <li>Place the spring rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.</li>
  <li>Brush the spring rolls with vegetable oil.</li>
  <li>Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.</li>
  <li>Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.</li>
</ol>
