<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Texas Sheet Cake Recipe</h2>
<p>Here are the ingredients you will need to make Texas sheet cake:</p>
<ul>
    <li>2 cups all-purpose flour</li>
    <li>2 cups granulated sugar</li>
    <li>1/2 cup unsalted butter</li>
    <li>1 cup water</li>
    <li>1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder</li>
    <li>1/2 cup buttermilk</li>
    <li>2 large eggs</li>
    <li>1 teaspoon baking soda</li>
    <li>1 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Texas Sheet Cake Recipe</h2>
<p>Follow these simple steps to make Texas sheet cake:</p>
<ol>
    <li>Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.</li>
    <li>In a medium saucepan, combine the butter, water, and cocoa powder. Bring the mixture to a boil, stirring constantly.</li>
    <li>In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt. Whisk to combine.</li>
    <li>Gradually add the cocoa mixture to the flour mixture, stirring until well combined.</li>
    <li>In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, eggs, and vanilla extract.</li>
    <li>Add the egg mixture to the flour mixture and stir until well combined.</li>
    <li>Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top with a spatula.</li>
    <li>Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.</li>
    <li>Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool for at least 10 minutes before frosting.</li>
</ol>
