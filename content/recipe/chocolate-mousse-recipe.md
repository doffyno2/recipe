<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Chocolate Mousse Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Chocolate%20Mousse%20Recipe%20Image&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Chocolate Mousse Recipe Image" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>To make this chocolate mousse recipe, you'll need the following ingredients:</p>
<ul>
  <li>8 ounces of semisweet chocolate, chopped</li>
  <li>4 tablespoons of unsalted butter, cut into small pieces</li>
  <li>4 large eggs, separated</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of granulated sugar</li>
  <li>1/8 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of heavy cream</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Chocolate Mousse Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Chocolate%20Mousse%20Recipe%20Image&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Chocolate Mousse Recipe Image" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Follow these steps to make this chocolate mousse recipe:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Fill a small saucepan with about 1 inch of water and bring it to a simmer over medium heat.</li>
  <li>Place the chopped chocolate and butter in the top of a double boiler and set it over the simmering water.</li>
  <li>Stir the chocolate and butter together until they are completely melted and smooth.</li>
  <li>Remove the chocolate mixture from the heat and let it cool slightly.</li>
  <li>Separate the eggs and place the egg yolks in a large mixing bowl and the egg whites in a separate mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>Add the granulated sugar and salt to the egg yolks and beat them with an electric mixer until they are light and fluffy.</li>
  <li>Slowly pour the melted chocolate mixture into the egg yolks and stir until they are well combined.</li>
  <li>In a separate mixing bowl, beat the egg whites with an electric mixer until they form stiff peaks.</li>
  <li>In a separate mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream with an electric mixer until it forms soft peaks.</li>
  <li>Using a spatula, gently fold the egg whites into the chocolate mixture until they are well combined.</li>
  <li>Next, gently fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture until it is well combined and no white streaks remain.</li>
  <li>Divide the chocolate mousse evenly among your serving cups or glasses and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until set.</li>
  <li>When ready to serve, garnish with whipped cream and shaved chocolate, if desired.</li>
</ol>
