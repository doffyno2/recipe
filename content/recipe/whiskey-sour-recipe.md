<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Whiskey Sour Recipe</h2>
<p>
To make a whiskey sour, you will need the following ingredients:
</p>
<ul>
  <li>2 ounces of whiskey</li>
  <li>1 ounce of freshly squeezed lemon juice</li>
  <li>1/2 ounce of simple syrup</li>
  <li>Ice cubes</li>
  <li>Garnish: orange slice and cherry</li>
</ul>
<p>
Note: You can use any type of whiskey you like, but bourbon is the classic choice for a whiskey sour.
</p>

<h2>How to Make Whiskey Sour Recipe</h2>
<p>
Follow these simple steps to make a whiskey sour:
</p>
<ol>
  <li>Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.</li>
  <li>Add 2 ounces of whiskey, 1 ounce of freshly squeezed lemon juice, and 1/2 ounce of simple syrup to the shaker.</li>
  <li>Shake vigorously for about 10 seconds.</li>
  <li>Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice cubes.</li>
  <li>Garnish with an orange slice and cherry.</li>
  <li>Serve and enjoy!</li>
</ol>
