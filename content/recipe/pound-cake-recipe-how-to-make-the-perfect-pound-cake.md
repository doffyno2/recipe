<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Pound Cake Recipe</h2>

<p>Pound cake is a classic dessert that has been enjoyed for generations. To make the perfect pound cake, you will need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
  <li>2 cups of all-purpose flour</li>
  <li>1 ½ cups of granulated sugar</li>
  <li>1 cup of unsalted butter (at room temperature)</li>
  <li>4 large eggs (at room temperature)</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of vanilla extract</li>
  <li>½ teaspoon of baking powder</li>
  <li>½ teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>½ cup of whole milk (at room temperature)</li>
</ul>

<p>Before we dive into how to make the perfect pound cake, let's talk about how to marinade it. Here are some ingredients you can use to create a delicious marinade:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 cup of powdered sugar</li>
  <li>½ cup of lemon juice</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of vanilla extract</li>
</ul>

<p>To create the marinade, simply mix all of the ingredients together in a bowl until smooth. Once the pound cake has been baked and cooled, pour the marinade over the cake and let it soak in for at least an hour before serving.</p>

<h2>How to Make Pound Cake Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Pound%20Cake%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Pound Cake Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have all of your ingredients and kitchen tools, it's time to make the perfect pound cake:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat your oven to 325°F.</li>
  <li>Prepare your loaf pan by lining it with parchment paper and spraying it with cooking spray.</li>
  <li>In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.</li>
  <li>Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.</li>
  <li>Mix in the vanilla extract.</li>
  <li>In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt.</li>
  <li>Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients in three parts, alternating with the milk and mixing well after each addition.</li>
  <li>Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth out the top with a spatula.</li>
  <li>Bake the pound cake for 60-70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.</li>
  <li>Let the pound cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing it from the pan and placing it on a wire rack to cool completely.</li>
  <li>Once the pound cake has cooled, you can pour the marinade over it and let it soak in for at least an hour before serving.</li>
</ol>
