<p>Before we get started, let's make sure we have all the ingredients needed to make snickerdoodle cookies:</p>

<ul>
  <li>2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour</li>
  <li>2 teaspoons cream of tartar</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon baking soda</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon salt</li>
  <li>1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature</li>
  <li>1 1/2 cups granulated sugar</li>
  <li>2 large eggs, at room temperature</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon vanilla extract</li>
  <li>1/4 cup granulated sugar (for rolling)</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons ground cinnamon (for rolling)</li>
</ul>

<p>Make sure you have all the ingredients on hand before getting started.</p>

<h2>How to Make Snickerdoodle Recipe</h2>

<p>Now that we have all the ingredients and kitchen tools ready, let's get started making the cookies:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).</li>
  <li>In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.</li>
  <li>In a large bowl, using an electric mixer, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.</li>
  <li>Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the butter mixture and mix until well combined.</li>
  <li>Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.</li>
  <li>In a small bowl, mix together the sugar and cinnamon for rolling.</li>
  <li>Roll the dough into 1 1/2 inch balls, then roll each ball in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.</li>
  <li>Place the cookies on a lined baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches between each cookie.</li>
  <li>Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden brown.</li>
  <li>Remove from the oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.</li>
</ol>
