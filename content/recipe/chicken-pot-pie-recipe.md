<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Chicken Pot Pie Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20For%20Chicken%20Pot%20Pie%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients For Chicken Pot Pie Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>For the crust:</p>
<ul>
<li>2 cups all-purpose flour</li>
<li>1 tsp salt</li>
<li>3/4 cup unsalted butter, cut into small cubes</li>
<li>1/2 cup ice water</li>
</ul>

<p>For the filling:</p>
<ul>
<li>1/2 cup unsalted butter</li>
<li>1/2 cup all-purpose flour</li>
<li>1/2 cup chopped onion</li>
<li>1/2 cup chopped celery</li>
<li>1/2 cup chopped carrots</li>
<li>1 tsp salt</li>
<li>1/2 tsp black pepper</li>
<li>1/4 tsp thyme</li>
<li>1/4 tsp sage</li>
<li>3 cups chicken broth</li>
<li>1 1/2 cups cooked, shredded chicken</li>
<li>1/2 cup frozen peas</li>
<li>1/2 cup frozen corn</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Chicken Pot Pie Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Chicken%20Pot%20Pie%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Chicken Pot Pie Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>For the crust:</p>
<ol>
<li>In a large bowl, mix together flour and salt.</li>
<li>Add in the butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to combine until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.</li>
<li>Gradually add in ice water, stirring until the dough comes together in a ball.</li>
<li>Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.</li>
</ol>

<p>For the filling:</p>
<ol>
<li>In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.</li>
<li>Add in the onion, celery, and carrots and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.</li>
<li>Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, thyme, and sage until combined.</li>
<li>Gradually stir in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a simmer.</li>
<li>Cook until the sauce has thickened, about 3-4 minutes.</li>
<li>Stir in the chicken, peas, and corn until heated through.</li>
</ol>

<p>Assembling the pie:</p>
<ol>
<li>Preheat the oven to 375°F.</li>
<li>Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface and transfer to a 9-inch pie dish.</li>
<li>Pour the chicken filling into the crust.</li>
<li>Roll out the remaining dough and place on top of the filling.</li>
<li>Crimp the edges of the crust together to seal the pie.</li>
<li>Cut several slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.</li>
<li>Place the pie on a baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes, until the crust is golden brown.</li>
</ol>
