<p>Minestrone soup is a hearty and healthy Italian classic that is perfect for any time of the year. This vegetable-based soup is packed with nutritious ingredients like beans, pasta, and fresh herbs, making it both delicious and wholesome.</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Minestrone Soup Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Minestrone%20Soup%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Minestrone Soup Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>The beauty of minestrone soup is that it is a flexible recipe that can be adjusted to suit your tastes and what ingredients you have on hand. However, here are the basic ingredients you will need to make a classic minestrone soup:</p>

<ul>
<li>2 tablespoons olive oil</li>
<li>1 onion, diced</li>
<li>2 cloves garlic, minced</li>
<li>2 carrots, peeled and diced</li>
<li>2 celery stalks, diced</li>
<li>1 zucchini, diced</li>
<li>1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes</li>
<li>1 can (15 ounces) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed</li>
<li>4 cups vegetable broth</li>
<li>1 cup small pasta (such as ditalini or elbow macaroni)</li>
<li>1 teaspoon dried oregano</li>
<li>1 teaspoon dried basil</li>
<li>Salt and pepper, to taste</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Minestrone Soup Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Minestrone%20Soup%20Cooking&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Minestrone Soup Cooking" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have gathered your ingredients and tools, it's time to make minestrone soup! Follow these simple steps:</p>

<ol>
<li>Heat the olive oil in a large soup pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.</li>
<li>Add the carrots, celery, and zucchini and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.</li>
<li>Stir in the diced tomatoes, cannellini beans, vegetable broth, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.</li>
<li>Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes.</li>
<li>Add the pasta and continue to simmer until it is tender, about 10 minutes.</li>
<li>Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.</li>
</ol>
