<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Frittata Recipe</h2>
<p>Before diving into how to make a delicious frittata, it’s important to have all the necessary ingredients. Here’s what you’ll need:</p>
<ul>
  <li>8 large eggs</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of milk or cream</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley or basil)</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons of olive oil</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of chopped vegetables (such as onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, or spinach)</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of cooked meat (such as ham or bacon)</li>
</ul>
<p>Feel free to customize the ingredients to your liking. Frittatas are a great way to use up leftover ingredients in your fridge!</p>

<h2>For the Marinade Frittata Recipe</h2>
<p>There’s no need to marinate a frittata since it’s a quick and easy dish to make. However, you can add more flavor to your frittata by sautéing your vegetables and meats in a little bit of oil or butter before adding the egg mixture. This will help caramelize the vegetables and bring out their natural sweetness.</p>

<h2>Kitchen Tools Needed for Frittata Recipe</h2>
<p>Here are the kitchen tools you’ll need to make a frittata:</p>
<ul>
  <li>A non-stick skillet (preferably oven-safe)</li>
  <li>A whisk or fork</li>
  <li>A spatula</li>
  <li>An oven (if your skillet is not oven-safe, you can use a baking dish instead)</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Frittata Recipe</h2>
<p>Now that you have all your ingredients and kitchen tools, it’s time to make a delicious frittata:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Preheat your oven to 375°F.</li>
  <li>In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk or cream, salt, pepper, parmesan cheese, and herbs.</li>
  <li>Heat the olive oil in your non-stick skillet over medium heat.</li>
  <li>Add your chopped vegetables and meat to the skillet and sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the meat is cooked through.</li>
  <li>Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables and meat in the skillet.</li>
  <li>Use a spatula to gently stir the mixture until the vegetables and meat are evenly distributed.</li>
  <li>Cook the frittata on the stovetop for 5 minutes, or until the bottom is set.</li>
  <li>Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the frittata is set and lightly golden on top.</li>
  <li>Remove the skillet from the oven and let the frittata cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Frittata Recipe</h2>
<p>Frittatas can be served hot or cold, making them a great breakfast or brunch dish that can be made ahead of time. To reheat a frittata, simply place it in the oven at 350°F for 10-15 minutes, or until heated through.</p>
