<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make the Best Sloppy Joe Recipe:</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20The%20Best%20Sloppy%20Joe%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make The Best Sloppy Joe Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>To make the best sloppy joe recipe, you'll need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 pound ground beef</li>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped onion</li>
  <li>1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper</li>
  <li>1/2 cup ketchup</li>
  <li>1/4 cup water</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon brown sugar</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon mustard</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon garlic powder</li>
  <li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade:</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=For%20The%20Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="For The Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before you start cooking, it's important to marinate the ground beef to give it extra flavor. Here's what you'll need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1/4 cup soy sauce</li>
  <li>1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons brown sugar</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon olive oil</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon garlic powder</li>
</ul>

<p>Combine all of the marinade ingredients in a bowl and whisk together. Place the ground beef in a Ziploc bag and pour the marinade over it. Seal the bag and massage the marinade into the meat. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours if you have the time.</p>

<h2>How to Make the Best Sloppy Joe Recipe:</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20The%20Best%20Sloppy%20Joe%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make The Best Sloppy Joe Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have all of your ingredients and kitchen tools ready, it's time to make the best sloppy joe recipe:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.</li>
  <li>Remove the ground beef from the marinade and discard the excess marinade.</li>
  <li>Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook, breaking it up with a wooden spoon, until it is browned and cooked through.</li>
  <li>Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.</li>
  <li>Add the chopped onion and green bell pepper to the skillet and cook until they are softened, about 5 minutes.</li>
  <li>Add the ketchup, water, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to the skillet and stir to combine.</li>
  <li>Return the beef to the skillet and stir to combine with the sauce.</li>
  <li>Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the flavors have melded together.</li>
  <li>Serve the sloppy joes on buns with your favorite toppings, such as shredded cheese, pickles, or onions.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook the Best Sloppy Joe Recipe:</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Cook%20The%20Best%20Sloppy%20Joe%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Cook The Best Sloppy Joe Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you know how to make the best sloppy joe recipe, it's time to cook it to perfection:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat your oven to 350°F.</li>
  <li>Place the buns on a baking sheet and toast them in the oven for 5-7 minutes, or until they are lightly browned.</li>
  <li>Remove the buns from the oven and place them on plates.</li>
  <li>Top each bun with a generous portion of the sloppy joe mixture.</li>
  <li>Add your favorite toppings, such as shredded cheese, pickles, or onions.</li>
  <li>Serve hot and enjoy!</li>
</ol>
