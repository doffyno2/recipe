<p>Golden milk is a traditional Indian drink that has gained popularity in recent years due to its numerous health benefits. It is made with a combination of spices and milk, and the main ingredient is turmeric, which gives it a beautiful golden color. This drink is not only delicious but also packed with antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties, making it an excellent addition to any diet. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive guide on how to make golden milk and answer some frequently asked questions.</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Golden Milk Recipe</h2>

<p>To make golden milk, you will need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
<li>1 cup of milk (you can use any type of milk, such as cow's milk, almond milk, or coconut milk)</li>
<li>1 teaspoon of ground turmeric</li>
<li>1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon</li>
<li>1/4 teaspoon of ground ginger</li>
<li>1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper</li>
<li>1/2 teaspoon of honey (optional)</li>
</ul>

<p>You can adjust the amount of spices and honey to your taste.</p>

<p>There is no need for a marinade in golden milk recipe.</p>

<h2>Kitchen Tools Needed Golden Milk Recipe</h2>

<p>You will need the following kitchen tools:</p>

<ul>
<li>Saucepan</li>
<li>Whisk</li>
<li>Measuring cups and spoons</li>
<li>Strainer (optional)</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Golden Milk Recipe</h2>

<p>Here are the steps to make golden milk:</p>

<ol>
<li>Pour the milk into a saucepan and heat it over medium heat.</li>
<li>Add the turmeric, cinnamon, ginger, and black pepper to the milk.</li>
<li>Whisk the milk and spices together until well combined.</li>
<li>Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for 5-10 minutes.</li>
<li>Stir in the honey, if using.</li>
<li>If desired, strain the golden milk through a strainer to remove any lumps or spices.</li>
<li>Pour the golden milk into a mug and enjoy!</li>
</ol>
