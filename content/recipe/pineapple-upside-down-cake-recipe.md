<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Pineapple Upside Down Cake Recipe</h2>

<p>Making pineapple upside down cake recipe is easy and requires a few ingredients that are easily available in the market.</p>

<ul>
<li>1 can of pineapple slices</li>
<li>1/3 cup of melted butter</li>
<li>1/2 cup of brown sugar</li>
<li>1/2 cup of white sugar</li>
<li>2 eggs</li>
<li>1 and 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour</li>
<li>2 tsp of baking powder</li>
<li>1/4 tsp of salt</li>
<li>3/4 cup of milk</li>
<li>1 tsp of vanilla extract</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Pineapple Upside Down Cake Recipe</h2>

<p>Follow these simple steps to make pineapple upside down cake recipe:</p>

<ol>
<li>Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.</li>
<li>Melt butter in a bowl and pour it into the cake pan.</li>
<li>Add brown sugar to the pan and mix well.</li>
<li>Arrange pineapple slices on top of the brown sugar mixture.</li>
<li>In a separate bowl, mix together the white sugar and eggs.</li>
<li>Add flour, baking powder, salt, milk, and vanilla extract to the bowl and mix well.</li>
<li>Pour the mixture on top of the pineapple slices.</li>
<li>Bake for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.</li>
<li>Remove from the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes.</li>
<li>Place a plate on top of the cake pan and flip it over.</li>
<li>Remove the cake pan and let it cool down completely.</li>
</ol>
