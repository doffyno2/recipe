<p>Are you craving for a hearty and flavorful Mexican soup that can easily warm you up on a cold day? Then look no further than this delicious taco soup recipe! Made with simple ingredients and straightforward instructions, this soup is perfect for a quick and satisfying meal that the whole family can enjoy.</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Taco Soup Recipe</h2>

<p>Before we dive into the recipe itself, let's take a look at the ingredients that you will need to make this mouth-watering taco soup:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 lb. ground beef</li>
  <li>1 can (15 oz.) kidney beans, drained and rinsed</li>
  <li>1 can (15 oz.) black beans, drained and rinsed</li>
  <li>1 can (15 oz.) corn, drained</li>
  <li>1 can (14.5 oz.) diced tomatoes, undrained</li>
  <li>1 can (10 oz.) diced tomatoes and green chilies, undrained</li>
  <li>1 can (8 oz.) tomato sauce</li>
  <li>1 packet (1 oz.) taco seasoning mix</li>
  <li>1 cup water</li>
  <li>Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, crushed tortilla chips, diced avocado, chopped cilantro, etc.</li>
</ul>

<p>With these ingredients ready, you're all set to start cooking!</p>

<h2>For the Marinade: Taco Soup Recipe</h2>

<p>There is no marinade needed for this recipe.</p>

<h2>Kitchen Tools Needed for Taco Soup Recipe</h2>

<p>Here are the basic kitchen tools that you'll need to make this taco soup recipe:</p>

<ul>
  <li>A large pot or Dutch oven, preferably with a lid</li>
  <li>A wooden spoon or spatula for stirring</li>
  <li>A can opener</li>
  <li>A measuring cup</li>
  <li>A sharp knife and cutting board for chopping vegetables, if needed</li>
</ul>

<p>With these tools in place, you're ready to start cooking this delicious soup!</p>

<h2>How to Make Taco Soup Recipe</h2>

<p>Now that you have all your ingredients and kitchen tools ready, let's start cooking this mouth-watering taco soup:</p>

<ol>
  <li>In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until no longer pink. Drain any excess fat.</li>
  <li>Add the kidney beans, black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, diced tomatoes and green chilies, tomato sauce, taco seasoning mix, and water to the pot, stirring to combine.</li>
  <li>Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.</li>
  <li>Remove the pot from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.</li>
  <li>Optional: top each bowl of soup with shredded cheese, sour cream, crushed tortilla chips, diced avocado, chopped cilantro, or any other toppings of your choice.</li>
</ol>

<p>That's it! Your delicious and hearty taco soup is now ready to serve. Enjoy!</p>

<h2>How to Cook Taco Soup Recipe</h2>

<p>The total cooking time for this taco soup recipe is about 30 minutes, including preparation and simmering. It can serve up to 6 people, depending on serving size and appetite.</p>
