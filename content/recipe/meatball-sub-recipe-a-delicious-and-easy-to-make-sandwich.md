<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Meatball Sub Recipe</h2>

<p>Meatball sub is a classic sandwich that is perfect for any occasion. To make a delicious meatball sub, you will need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 pound of ground beef</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of breadcrumbs</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese</li>
  <li>1 egg</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of milk</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of onion powder</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of chopped fresh basil</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of chopped fresh oregano</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of marinara sauce</li>
  <li>4 sub rolls</li>
  <li>1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Meatball Sub Recipe</h2>

<p>To make the marinade for the meatballs, you will need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1/4 cup of olive oil</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of honey</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard</li>
  <li>1 garlic clove, minced</li>
</ul>

<h2>Kitchen Tools Needed for Meatball Sub Recipe</h2>

<p>To make the meatball sub recipe, you will need the following kitchen tools:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Large mixing bowl</li>
  <li>Baking sheet</li>
  <li>Large skillet</li>
  <li>Tongs</li>
  <li>Cutting board</li>

<h2>How to Make Meatball Sub Recipe</h2>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat the oven to 375°F.</li>
  <li>In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, milk, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, parsley, basil, and oregano. Mix until well combined.</li>
  <li>Form the mixture into 1 1/2 inch meatballs and place them on a baking sheet.</li>
  <li>Bake the meatballs in the oven for 20 minutes or until they are browned and cooked through.</li>
  <li>While the meatballs are cooking, make the marinade. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, and garlic.</li>
  <li>When the meatballs are done, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the marinara sauce and the meatballs to the skillet and toss to coat the meatballs in the sauce.</li>
  <li>Cut the sub rolls in half and place them on a baking sheet. Top each half with some shredded mozzarella cheese.</li>
  <li>Toast the sub rolls in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, about 5 minutes.</li>
  <li>Using tongs, place the meatballs on the bottom half of each sub roll. Drizzle the marinade over the meatballs and top with the other half of the sub roll.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Meatball Sub Recipe</h2>

<p>Cooking meatball sub recipe is easy and straightforward. Follow these simple steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat your oven to 375°F.</li>
  <li>In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, milk, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, parsley, basil, and oregano. Mix until well combined.</li>
  <li>Form the mixture into 1 1/2 inch meatballs and place them on a baking sheet. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes or until they are browned and cooked through.</li>
  <li>While the meatballs are cooking, make the marinade. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, and garlic.</li>
  <li>When the meatballs are done, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the marinara sauce and the meatballs to the skillet and toss to coat the meatballs in the sauce.</li>
  <li>Cut the sub rolls in half and place them on a baking sheet. Top each half with some shredded mozzarella cheese.</li>
  <li>Toast the sub rolls in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, about 5 minutes.</li>
  <li>Using tongs, place the meatballs on the bottom half of each sub roll. Drizzle the marinade over the meatballs and top with the other half of the sub roll.</li>
  <li>Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade meatball sub!</li>
</ol>
