<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Caramel Roll Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Caramel%20Roll%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Caramel Roll Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before you begin making caramel rolls, it's important to gather all the necessary ingredients. Here's what you'll need:</p>

<ul>
    <li>Flour (3 cups)</li>
    <li>Sugar (1/4 cup)</li>
    <li>Salt (1 tsp)</li>
    <li>Yeast (1 package)</li>
    <li>Milk (1/2 cup)</li>
    <li>Butter (1/4 cup)</li>
    <li>Egg (1 large)</li>
    <li>Corn syrup (1/4 cup)</li>
    <li>Brown sugar (1 cup)</li>
    <li>Vanilla extract (1 tsp)</li>
    <li>Cinnamon (1 tsp)</li>
    <li>Chopped pecans (1 cup)</li>
    <li>Caramel sauce (1 cup)</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Caramel Roll Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=For%20The%20Marinade%20Caramel%20Roll%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="For The Marinade Caramel Roll Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have all the ingredients ready, it's time to get started on the marinade. Here's what you need to do:</p>

<ol>
    <li>In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Mix well.</li>
    <li>In a separate bowl, heat milk and butter until warm. Add egg and corn syrup to the mixture, and then pour into the flour mixture. Mix until everything is well combined.</li>
    <li>Knead the dough on a floured surface for several minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.</li>
    <li>Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about an hour.</li>
    <li>After an hour, punch down the dough and roll it out into a rectangle shape.</li>
    <li>In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar, cinnamon, and chopped pecans. Sprinkle the mixture over the dough.</li>
    <li>Roll up the dough tightly, and then cut it into 12 equal pieces.</li>
    <li>Place the pieces into a greased baking dish, cover it with a towel, and let it rise for another hour.</li>
    <li>Preheat the oven to 350째F. Bake the rolls for 25-30 minutes or until they are golden brown.</li>
    <li>Remove the rolls from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.</li>
    <li>Drizzle caramel sauce over the rolls, and then serve warm.</li>
</ol>
