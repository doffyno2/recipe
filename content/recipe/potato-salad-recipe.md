<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Potato Salad Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=For%20The%20Marinade%20Potato%20Salad%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="For The Marinade Potato Salad Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Before we begin, let's go over the ingredients you'll need to make potato salad:</p>
<ul>
  <li>3 pounds of potatoes</li>
  <li>1 cup of mayonnaise</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of yellow mustard</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of chopped red onion</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of chopped celery</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of chopped dill pickles</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Potato Salad Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Kitchen%20Tools%20Needed%20Potato%20Salad%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Kitchen Tools Needed Potato Salad Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Now that you have all the ingredients, it's time to prepare the marinade:</p>
<ul>
  <li>In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, yellow mustard, apple cider vinegar, chopped red onion, chopped celery, chopped dill pickles, chopped fresh parsley, salt, and black pepper.</li>
  <li>Cover the marinade and refrigerate it for at least 30 minutes.</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Potato Salad Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Cook%20Potato%20Salad%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Cook Potato Salad Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and kitchen tools, it's time to make the potato salad:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Wash the potatoes and cut them into bite-sized pieces.</li>
  <li>Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover them with water.</li>
  <li>Bring the water to a boil over high heat.</li>
  <li>Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer the potatoes for 15-20 minutes, or until they are tender.</li>
  <li>Drain the potatoes and let them cool for 10-15 minutes.</li>
  <li>In a large mixing bowl, combine the potatoes and the marinade.</li>
  <li>Mix the ingredients until the potatoes are evenly coated with the marinade.</li>
  <li>Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the salad for at least 1 hour before serving.</li>
</ol>
