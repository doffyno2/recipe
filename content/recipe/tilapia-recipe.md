<h2>Ingredients Needed</h2>
<p>Before we start, let's gather all the ingredients we need:</p>
<ul>
<li>4 tilapia fillets</li>
<li>1/4 cup olive oil</li>
<li>2 tbsp. lemon juice</li>
<li>1 tbsp. honey</li>
<li>1 tsp. garlic powder</li>
<li>1 tsp. onion powder</li>
<li>1 tsp. dried oregano</li>
<li>1 tsp. dried basil</li>
<li>1/2 tsp. salt</li>
<li>1/2 tsp. black pepper</li>
<li>1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper</li>
<li>1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped</li>
</ul>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20For%20Tilapia%20Recipe%20Image&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients For Tilapia Recipe Image" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<h2>Marinade</h2>
<p>First, we need to prepare the marinade:</p>
<ul>
<li>In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, honey, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, basil, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper.</li>
<li>Place the tilapia fillets in a large ziplock bag and pour the marinade over them.</li>
<li>Seal the bag and gently shake it to make sure the marinade is evenly distributed.</li>
<li>Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours.</li>
</ul>

</center>

<h2>Kitchen Tools Needed</h2>
<p>Now that we have our ingredients and marinade ready, let's gather the kitchen tools we need:</p>
<ul>
<li>Large ziplock bag</li>
<li>Small bowl</li>
<li>Whisk</li>
<li>Frying pan or grill</li>

<h2>How to Make Tilapia Recipe</h2>
<p>After gathering all the necessary ingredients and tools, it's time to make the tilapia recipe:</p>
<ol>
<li>Preheat the frying pan or grill over medium-high heat.</li>
<li>Remove the tilapia fillets from the ziplock bag, allowing any excess marinade to drip off.</li>
<li>Place the fillets on the frying pan or grill and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side or until they are cooked through and flaky.</li>
<li>Once the fillets are cooked, remove them from the heat and transfer them onto a plate.</li>
<li>Garnish the tilapia fillets with chopped parsley and serve immediately with your favorite sides.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Tilapia Recipe</h2>
<p>Now that we know how to make the tilapia recipe, let's learn how to cook it:</p>
<ol>
<li>If you're using a frying pan, preheat it over medium-high heat. If you're using a grill, preheat it to medium-high.</li>
<li>Remove the tilapia fillets from the marinade and let any excess marinade drip off.</li>
<li>Place the fillets on the frying pan or grill and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side or until they are cooked through and flaky.</li>
<li>Once the fillets are cooked, remove them from the heat and transfer them onto a plate.</li>
<li>Garnish the tilapia fillets with chopped parsley and serve immediately with your favorite sides.</li>
</ol>
