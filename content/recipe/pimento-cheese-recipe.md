<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Pimento Cheese Recipe</h2>

<p>To make a delicious pimento cheese recipe, you will need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
<li>2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese</li>
<li>1 cup of shredded Monterey Jack cheese</li>
<li>1/2 cup of mayonnaise</li>
<li>1/4 cup of diced pimentos</li>
<li>1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder</li>
<li>1/4 teaspoon of onion powder</li>
<li>1/4 teaspoon of salt</li>
<li>1/4 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Pimento Cheese Recipe</h2>

<p>Follow these simple steps to make a delicious pimento cheese recipe:</p>

<ol>
<li>Grate the cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese and place both in a bowl.</li>
<li>Add mayonnaise, diced pimentos, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper to the bowl and mix well until all ingredients are combined.</li>
<li>Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.</li>
</ol>
