<p>Butter chicken is a classic Indian dish that everyone loves. It is a creamy, flavorful, and delicious dish that is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about making butter chicken at home. We will cover the ingredients, the marinade, the kitchen tools you need, and the step-by-step instructions on how to make this mouth-watering dish. So, let's get started!</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Butter Chicken Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Butter%20Chicken%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Butter Chicken Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before we get started, let's gather all the ingredients we need to make this delicious dish:</p>
<ul>
<li>1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces</li>
<li>1 cup plain yogurt</li>
<li>2 tablespoons lemon juice</li>
<li>2 teaspoons ground cumin</li>
<li>2 teaspoons paprika</li>
<li>1 teaspoon turmeric</li>
<li>1 teaspoon garam masala</li>
<li>1 teaspoon salt</li>
<li>1/2 teaspoon black pepper</li>
<li>1/2 cup unsalted butter</li>
<li>1 onion, finely chopped</li>
<li>3 garlic cloves, minced</li>
<li>1 tablespoon grated ginger</li>
<li>1 teaspoon ground cumin</li>
<li>1 teaspoon paprika</li>
<li>1 teaspoon garam masala</li>
<li>1 (14-ounce) can crushed tomatoes</li>
<li>1/2 cup heavy cream</li>
<li>1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Butter Chicken Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=For%20The%20Marinade%20Butter%20Chicken%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="For The Marinade Butter Chicken Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that we have all the ingredients, let's start with the marinade:</p>
<ol>
<li>In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and black pepper.</li>
<li>Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or up to 24 hours.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Make Butter Chicken Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Butter%20Chicken%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Butter Chicken Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that we have everything we need, let's start cooking:</p>
<ol>
<li>In a large skillet or Dutch oven, melt the butter over medium-high heat.</li>
<li>Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, and cook until softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.</li>
<li>Add the cumin, paprika, and garam masala, and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.</li>
<li>Add the crushed tomatoes and heavy cream, and stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer.</li>
<li>Add the marinated chicken pieces to the skillet and stir to combine. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through.</li>
<li>Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.</li>
</ol>
