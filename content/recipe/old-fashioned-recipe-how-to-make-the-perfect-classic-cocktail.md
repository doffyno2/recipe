<h2>Introduction</h2>

Are you a fan of classic cocktails? If so, then the Old Fashioned should be at the top of your list. This timeless drink has been around since the early 1800s and has stood the test of time. With its perfect balance of sweetness and bitterness, it's no wonder why the Old Fashioned remains a popular cocktail. In this article, we'll teach you everything you need to know to make the perfect Old Fashioned recipe.

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Old Fashioned Recipe</h2>

Before we get started, let's go over the ingredients you'll need to make the perfect Old Fashioned recipe. Here's what you'll need:

- 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
- 1 sugar cube or 1 tsp sugar
- 2 dashes Angostura bitters
- 1 orange slice
- 1 maraschino cherry
- Ice
- Club soda (optional)

<h2>Kitchen Tools Needed Old Fashioned Recipe</h2>

To make an Old Fashioned recipe, you'll need a few kitchen tools. Here's what you'll need:

- Jigger or measuring cup

<h2>How to Make Old Fashioned Recipe</h2>

Now that you have all the ingredients and tools you need, it's time to make the perfect Old Fashioned recipe. Follow these steps:

1. Place the sugar cube or sugar in the bottom of the rocks glass.
2. Add the Angostura bitters on top of the sugar.
3. Add a splash of club soda (optional) and muddle everything together until the sugar is dissolved.
4. Fill the glass with ice.
5. Add the whiskey and stir everything together with a bar spoon or regular spoon.
6. Garnish with an orange slice and a maraschino cherry.
