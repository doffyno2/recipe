<p>While Negroni is typically served as a cocktail, it can also be used as a marinade for meat or fish. Here's how to make a Negroni marinade:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Combine 1 oz gin, 1 oz sweet vermouth, and 1 oz Campari in a bowl.</li>
  <li>Add 1 lb of your preferred meat or fish to the bowl, making sure it is fully coated in the marinade.</li>
  <li>Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.</li>
  <li>Remove the meat or fish from the marinade and discard the excess liquid.</li>
  <li>Cook the meat or fish according to your preferred method.</li>
</ul>

<p>The Negroni marinade adds a unique and delicious flavor to meat and fish dishes. Give it a try at your next dinner party!</p>
