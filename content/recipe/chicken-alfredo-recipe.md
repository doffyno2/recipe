<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Chicken Alfredo Recipe</h2>

<p>Chicken alfredo is one of the most popular pasta dishes, loved by people all over the world. To make this delicious recipe, you'll need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts</li>
  <li>1 pound of fettuccine pasta</li>
  <li>1 cup of heavy cream</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of unsalted butter</li>
  <li>4 cloves of garlic, minced</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons of olive oil</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade</h2>

<p>The marinade is an essential component of the chicken alfredo recipe. Here's what you'll need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1/4 cup of olive oil</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons of lemon juice</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon of Italian seasoning</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<h2>How to Make Chicken Alfredo Recipe</h2>

<p>Follow these simple steps to make the perfect chicken alfredo:</p>

<ol>
  <li>In a mixing bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper to make the marinade. Add the chicken breasts to the marinade and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.</li>
  <li>Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.</li>
  <li>In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken breasts and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side, until fully cooked. Remove from the skillet and set aside.</li>
  <li>In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.</li>
  <li>Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream to the skillet. Stir constantly until the cream starts to simmer.</li>
  <li>Add the grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.</li>
  <li>Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet and toss to coat with the sauce. Slice the cooked chicken breasts and place on top of the pasta.</li>
  <li>Serve hot and enjoy!</li>
</ol>
