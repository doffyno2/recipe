<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Banana Pancakes Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Banana%20Pancakes%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Banana Pancakes Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before you start making the banana pancakes, make sure you have all the necessary ingredients:</p>

<ul>
<li>1 cup all-purpose flour</li>
<li>1 tablespoon sugar</li>
<li>2 teaspoons baking powder</li>
<li>1/4 teaspoon salt</li>
<li>1 ripe banana, mashed</li>
<li>1 egg</li>
<li>1 cup milk</li>
<li>1 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
<li>Butter or oil for cooking</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Banana Pancakes Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Banana%20Pancakes%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Banana Pancakes Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and kitchen tools ready, it's time to make banana pancakes! Follow these steps:</p>

<ol>
<li>In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.</li>
<li>In a separate bowl, mix mashed banana, egg, milk, and vanilla extract.</li>
<li>Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until combined.</li>
<li>Heat a frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.</li>
<li>Using a ladle, scoop batter onto the frying pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.</li>
<li>Flip and cook for an additional minute or until golden brown.</li>
<li>Repeat with remaining batter.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Banana Pancakes Recipe</h2>

</center>

<p>Once you've made the banana pancake batter, it's time to cook them! Follow these steps:</p>

<ol>
<li>Heat a frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.</li>
<li>Using a ladle, scoop batter onto the frying pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.</li>
<li>Flip and cook for an additional minute or until golden brown.</li>
<li>Repeat with remaining batter.</li>
</ol>
