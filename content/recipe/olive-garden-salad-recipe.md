<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Olive Garden Salad Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Olive%20Garden%20Salad%20Recipe%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Olive Garden Salad Recipe Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>The first step to making Olive Garden salad is to gather the necessary ingredients. Here are the ingredients you need to make the salad:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 head of romaine lettuce</li>
  <li>1/2 head of iceberg lettuce</li>
  <li>10-12 baby carrots</li>
  <li>1/2 red onion</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of sliced black olives</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of croutons</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Olive%20Garden%20Salad%20Recipe%20Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Olive Garden Salad Recipe Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>The marinade is what makes the Olive Garden salad unique. Here are the ingredients you need to make the marinade:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1/2 cup of olive oil</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of white wine vinegar</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of water</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons of mayonnaise</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of dried basil</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of black pepper</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Olive Garden Salad Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Olive%20Garden%20Salad%20Recipe%20How%20To%20Make&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Olive Garden Salad Recipe How To Make" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have gathered all the ingredients and kitchen tools, it's time to make the salad. Here are the steps to follow:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Chop the romaine lettuce and iceberg lettuce into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>Peel the baby carrots and slice them into thin rounds. Add them to the mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>Peel and slice the red onion into thin pieces. Add them to the mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>Add the sliced black olives, croutons, and grated parmesan cheese to the mixing bowl.</li>
  <li>In a separate bowl, whisk together the olive oil, white wine vinegar, water, mayonnaise, grated parmesan cheese, garlic powder, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to make the marinade.</li>
  <li>Pour the marinade over the salad and toss everything together until the salad is evenly coated with the marinade.</li>
  <li>Let the salad sit in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.</li>
  <li>Take the salad out of the refrigerator, give it a final toss, and serve.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Olive Garden Salad Recipe</h2>

<p>Olive Garden salad is a cold salad that doesn't require any cooking. Simply follow the steps above to make the salad and enjoy it as is.</p>
