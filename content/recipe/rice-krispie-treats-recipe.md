<p>If you're looking for a quick and easy dessert recipe that's sure to please everyone, then look no further than rice krispie treats! These delicious treats are a classic favorite and can be made with just a few simple ingredients. In this article, we'll show you how to make rice krispie treats from scratch and provide tips on how to make them even more delicious.</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Rice Krispie Treats Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Rice%20Krispies%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Rice Krispies Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>The following ingredients are needed to make rice krispie treats:</p>

<ul>
  <li>6 cups of Rice Krispies cereal</li>
  <li>4 tablespoons of butter</li>
  <li>1 package of marshmallows (10 ounces)</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Rice Krispie Treats Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Rice%20Krispie%20Treats%20Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Rice Krispie Treats Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>While you can certainly enjoy plain rice krispie treats, adding a marinade can take them to the next level. Here's a simple marinade recipe:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1/4 cup of peanut butter</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of honey</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of vanilla extract</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of salt</li>
</ul>

<p>Simply mix together all the ingredients in a bowl, then drizzle the marinade over the rice krispie treats once they've cooled.</p>

<h2>How to Make Rice Krispie Treats Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Rice%20Krispie%20Treats%20How%20To%20Make&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Rice Krispie Treats How To Make" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Follow these steps to make delicious rice krispie treats:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Melt the butter in a large saucepan over low heat.</li>
  <li>Add the marshmallows to the saucepan and stir until they're completely melted.</li>
  <li>Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the vanilla extract (if desired).</li>
  <li>Add the Rice Krispies cereal to the saucepan and stir until they're completely coated with the marshmallow mixture.</li>
  <li>Transfer the mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish and press it down evenly with a spatula.</li>
  <li>Allow the mixture to cool for at least 30 minutes before cutting it into squares and serving.</li>
</ol>
