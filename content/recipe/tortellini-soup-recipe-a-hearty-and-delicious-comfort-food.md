<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Tortellini Soup Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Tortellini%20Soup%20Recipe%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Tortellini Soup Recipe Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before we dive into the recipe, let's first take a look at the ingredients you will need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 pound of tortellini</li>
  <li>6 cups of chicken broth</li>
  <li>1 can of diced tomatoes</li>
  <li>1 medium onion, chopped</li>
  <li>3 cloves of garlic, minced</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons of olive oil</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon of dried oregano</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon of dried basil</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes</li>
  <li>Salt and pepper, to taste</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley</li>
  <li>Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Tortellini Soup Recipe</h2>

<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and tools, let's start making the soup:</p>

<ol>
  <li>In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.</li>
  <li>Add the chopped onions and cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they are translucent.</li>
  <li>Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, or until fragrant.</li>
  <li>Add the chicken broth, diced tomatoes, oregano, basil, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Bring the soup to a boil.</li>
  <li>Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10-15 minutes.</li>
  <li>Add the tortellini and cook for another 8-10 minutes, or until they are tender.</li>
  <li>Stir in the chopped parsley.</li>
  <li>Ladle the soup into bowls and top with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.</li>
  <li>Serve hot and enjoy!</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Tortellini Soup Recipe</h2>

<p>Cooking tortellini soup is easy and straightforward. Just follow these simple steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Boil the tortellini in a separate pot of salted water for 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender.</li>
  <li>Drain the tortellini and rinse them under cold water to stop the cooking process.</li>
  <li>Add the cooked tortellini to the soup and simmer for a few minutes until heated through.</li>
  <li>Follow the rest of the recipe as instructed.</li>
</ol>
