<h2>Introduction</h2>

Are you tired of the same old burger patties? Why not try making a juicy and flavorful hamburger steak? This dish is perfect for those who want to elevate their burger game. We will cover the ingredients needed, the marinade recipe, the kitchen tools required, and the steps to cook and make the perfect hamburger steak.

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Hamburger Steak Recipe</h2>

To make the perfect hamburger steak, you will need the following ingredients:

<ul>
    <li>1 pound ground beef</li>
    <li>1/4 cup breadcrumbs</li>
    <li>1/4 cup milk</li>
    <li>1 egg</li>
    <li>1/4 cup chopped onions</li>
    <li>1/4 cup chopped green onions</li>
    <li>1 tablespoon garlic powder</li>
    <li>1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce</li>
    <li>1 teaspoon salt</li>
    <li>1/2 teaspoon black pepper</li>
</ul>

<h2>Marinade Recipe for Hamburger Steak Recipe</h2>

The marinade recipe for the hamburger steak is as follows:

<ul>
    <li>1/4 cup soy sauce</li>
    <li>1/4 cup brown sugar</li>
    <li>1/4 cup olive oil</li>
    <li>1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce</li>
    <li>1 tablespoon minced garlic</li>
    <li>1 tablespoon Dijon mustard</li>
</ul>

Mix all the ingredients in a bowl and stir until the sugar has dissolved. Pour the marinade over the hamburger steak and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes before cooking.

<h2>Kitchen Tools Needed to Make Hamburger Steak Recipe</h2>

To make the perfect hamburger steak, you will need the following kitchen tools:

<ul>
    <li>Large mixing bowl</li>
    <li>Whisk</li>
    <li>Measuring cups and spoons</li>
    <li>Frying pan or grill</li>

<h2>How to Make Hamburger Steak Recipe</h2>

Now that you have all the ingredients and kitchen tools ready, it's time to make the perfect hamburger steak. Follow these steps:

<ol>
    <li>In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, chopped onions, chopped green onions, garlic powder, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and black pepper. Mix well using your hands or a whisk.</li>
    <li>Divide the mixture into 4-6 equal portions and shape them into oval-shaped patties.</li>
    <li>Pour the marinade over the patties and let them marinate for at least 30 minutes.</li>
    <li>Preheat the frying pan or grill on medium-high heat.</li>
    <li>Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side or until they are cooked through.</li>
    <li>Remove the patties from the pan or grill and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Hamburger Steak Recipe</h2>

To cook the perfect hamburger steak, follow these steps:

<ol>
    <li>Preheat the frying pan or grill on medium-high heat.</li>
    <li>Remove the patties from the marinade and shake off any excess.</li>
    <li>Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side or until they are cooked through.</li>
    <li>Remove the patties from the pan or grill and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.</li>
</ol>
