<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Oxtail Soup Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Oxtail%20Soup%20Ingredients%20Image&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Oxtail Soup Ingredients Image" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>To make oxtail soup, you'll need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
<li>2-3 pounds of oxtail</li>
<li>1 onion, chopped</li>
<li>4 garlic cloves, minced</li>
<li>2 carrots, chopped</li>
<li>2 celery stalks, chopped</li>
<li>2 bay leaves</li>
<li>1 tablespoon of tomato paste</li>
<li>8 cups of beef broth</li>
<li>1 tablespoon of olive oil</li>
<li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Oxtail Soup Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Oxtail%20Soup%20Marinade%20Image&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Oxtail Soup Marinade Image" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before cooking the oxtail, you'll need to marinate it. Here's what you'll need for the marinade:</p>

<ul>
<li>1/4 cup of soy sauce</li>
<li>1/4 cup of Worcestershire sauce</li>
<li>1/4 cup of balsamic vinegar</li>
<li>2 tablespoons of brown sugar</li>
<li>2 bay leaves</li>
<li>1 tablespoon of black peppercorns</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Oxtail Soup Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Oxtail%20Soup%20Image&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Oxtail Soup Image" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and kitchen tools ready, it's time to make the oxtail soup. Follow these steps:</p>

<ol>
<li>In a mixing bowl, combine all the marinade ingredients and mix well.</li>
<li>Add the oxtail to the bowl and coat well with the marinade. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it marinate in the fridge for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.</li>
<li>Once the oxtail has marinated, heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the oxtail and cook until browned on all sides, using tongs or a slotted spoon to turn the pieces.</li>
<li>Remove the oxtail from the pot and set it aside on a plate.</li>
<li>Add the onions, garlic, carrots, and celery to the pot and cook until the vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.</li>
<li>Add the tomato paste and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.</li>
<li>Add the beef broth and bay leaves to the pot, and bring the soup to a boil.</li>
<li>Add the oxtail back to the pot, reduce the heat to low, and let the soup simmer for 2-3 hours, or until the oxtail is tender and falling off the bone.</li>
<li>Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.</li>
<li>Remove the bay leaves and serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.</li>
</ol>
