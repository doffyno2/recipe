<p>Are you looking for a delicious and easy pork loin recipe that will impress your family and friends? Look no further! In this article, we will show you how to make and cook the perfect pork loin that will leave everyone satisfied and craving for more.</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Pork Loin Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Pork%20Loin%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Pork Loin Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>For this pork loin recipe, you will need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 boneless pork loin (3-4 pounds)</li>
  <li>1/2 cup soy sauce</li>
  <li>1/2 cup brown sugar</li>
  <li>1/4 cup olive oil</li>
  <li>1/4 cup apple cider vinegar</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon garlic powder</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon onion powder</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon dried oregano</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon dried thyme</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon black pepper</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Pork Loin Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=For%20The%20Marinade%20Pork%20Loin%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="For The Marinade Pork Loin Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>To make the marinade for the pork loin recipe, combine the soy sauce, brown sugar, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder, dried oregano, dried thyme, and black pepper in a large bowl. Mix well until the sugar is dissolved.</p>

<p>Place the pork loin in a large resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over it. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight for best results.</p>

<h2>How to Make Pork Loin Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Pork%20Loin%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Pork Loin Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Follow these easy steps to make the perfect pork loin:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat oven to 375°F. </li>
  <li>Remove the pork loin from the marinade and place it in a baking dish. </li>
  <li>Bake the pork loin for 1 hour and 30 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F. Use a meat thermometer to check the temperature. </li>
  <li>Remove the pork loin from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes. </li>
  <li>Wrap the pork loin in aluminum foil and let it rest for an additional 10 minutes. </li>
  <li>Slice the pork loin and serve with your favorite sides. </li>
</ol>
