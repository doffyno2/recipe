<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Chicken Quesadilla Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Chicken%20Quesadilla%20Recipe%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Chicken Quesadilla Recipe Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>For this recipe, you will need the following ingredients:</p>
<ul>
  <li>2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of your favorite marinade</li>
  <li>4 large flour tortillas</li>
  <li>2 cups of shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey jack)</li>
  <li>1 bell pepper, sliced</li>
  <li>1 onion, sliced</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon olive oil</li>
  <li>Sour cream (optional)</li>
  <li>Salsa (optional)</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Chicken Quesadilla Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Chicken%20Quesadilla%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Chicken Quesadilla Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Follow these steps to make chicken quesadillas:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Place chicken breasts in a bowl with marinade and let sit for at least 30 minutes or up to overnight in the refrigerator.</li>
  <li>Preheat grill or grill pan on medium-high heat.</li>
  <li>Remove chicken breasts from marinade and grill for 6-7 minutes per side or until cooked through.</li>
  <li>Remove chicken from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing into thin strips.</li>
  <li>While chicken is resting, heat saute pan over medium heat and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil.</li>
  <li>Add sliced onions and bell peppers to the pan and saute until tender, about 5-7 minutes.</li>
  <li>Remove onions and bell peppers from the pan and set aside.</li>
  <li>Place one tortilla in the same saute pan and sprinkle 1/2 cup of shredded cheese on top.</li>
  <li>Add a layer of sliced chicken, onions, and bell peppers on top of the cheese.</li>
  <li>Sprinkle another 1/2 cup of shredded cheese on top of the chicken mixture and place another tortilla on top.</li>
  <li>Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy.</li>
  <li>Remove from the pan and cut into quarters.</li>
  <li>Repeat the process for the remaining tortillas and chicken mixture.</li>
  <li>Serve with sour cream and salsa if desired and enjoy!</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Chicken Quesadilla Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Cook%20Chicken%20Quesadilla%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Cook Chicken Quesadilla Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>To cook chicken quesadillas, follow these steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Heat saute pan over medium heat and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil.</li>
  <li>Add one tortilla to the pan and sprinkle 1/2 cup of shredded cheese on top.</li>
  <li>Add a layer of sliced chicken, onions, and bell peppers on top of the cheese.</li>
  <li>Sprinkle another 1/2 cup of shredded cheese on top of the chicken mixture and place another tortilla on top.</li>
  <li>Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy.</li>
  <li>Remove from the pan and cut into quarters.</li>
  <li>Repeat the process for the remaining tortillas and chicken mixture.</li>
  <li>Serve with sour cream and salsa if desired and enjoy!</li>
</ol>
