<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Baked Brie Recipe</h2>

<p>For this recipe, you will need:</p>

<ul>
    <li>1 wheel of brie cheese</li>
    <li>1 sheet of puff pastry</li>
    <li>1 egg, beaten</li>
    <li>1/4 cup of your favorite jam or preserves</li>
    <li>1/4 cup of chopped nuts (optional)</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Baked Brie Recipe</h2>

<p>There is no marinade needed for this recipe, but you can add some honey or herbs to the top of the brie before baking for added flavor.</p>

<h2>How to Make Baked Brie Recipe</h2>

<p>Follow these simple steps to make this delicious baked brie recipe:</p>

<ol>
    <li>Preheat your oven to 375Â°F.</li>
    <li>Unroll the puff pastry sheet and place the brie cheese in the center.</li>
    <li>Spread the jam or preserves on top of the brie cheese.</li>
    <li>Sprinkle chopped nuts on top (optional).</li>
    <li>Wrap the puff pastry around the brie cheese, making sure to seal the edges.</li>
    <li>Place the wrapped brie cheese on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.</li>
    <li>Brush the beaten egg on top of the puff pastry.</li>
    <li>Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the puff pastry is golden brown.</li>
    <li>Let the baked brie cheese cool for a few minutes before serving with crackers or sliced baguette.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Baked Brie Recipe</h2>

<p>Follow the instructions above to cook this delicious baked brie recipe. The key is to make sure that the puff pastry is golden brown and the cheese is melted and gooey.</p>
