<h2>How to Make Chicken and Waffles Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Chicken%20And%20Waffles%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Chicken And Waffles Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>
Here are the steps to make the perfect chicken and waffles recipe:
</p>
<ol>
    <li>In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.</li>
    <li>In a separate mixing bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and melted butter.</li>
    <li>Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and whisk until the batter is smooth.</li>
    <li>Preheat your waffle maker according to the manufacturer's instructions.</li>
    <li>Pour the batter into the waffle maker and cook until golden brown and crispy.</li>
    <li>In another mixing bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, hot sauce, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper to make the marinade.</li>
    <li>Add the chicken breasts to the marinade and let sit for at least 30 minutes.</li>
    <li>Preheat your skillet or deep fryer with vegetable oil to 350°F.</li>
    <li>Remove the chicken breasts from the marinade and let any excess drip off.</li>
    <li>Fry the chicken in the hot oil until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side.</li>
    <li>Place the cooked chicken on a wire rack to drain any excess oil.</li>
    <li>Serve the chicken with the waffles and maple syrup.</li>
</ol>
