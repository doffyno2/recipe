<p>If you are a fan of the classic chocolate chip cookies, then you must try the Nestle chocolate chip cookies recipe. These cookies are easy to make, and they turn out perfectly chewy and moist every time. In this article, we will guide you through the ingredients, marinade, kitchen tools needed, and the step-by-step process of making and cooking the Nestle chocolate chip cookies recipe. </p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Nestle Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe</h2>

<p>The ingredients for this recipe are simple and easy to find. Here is what you need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour</li>
  <li>1 tsp baking soda</li>
  <li>1 tsp salt</li>
  <li>1 cup unsalted butter, softened</li>
  <li>3/4 cup white granulated sugar</li>
  <li>3/4 cup brown sugar, firmly packed</li>
  <li>2 large eggs</li>
  <li>2 tsp vanilla extract</li>
  <li>2 cups Nestle Toll House Semi-Sweet Chocolate Morsels</li>
</ul>

<p>Before we get started with the recipe, let's talk about the marinade. You can customize the flavor of your cookies by adding different ingredients to the marinade. Here are some suggestions:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)</li>
  <li>1/2 cup shredded coconut</li>
  <li>1/2 cup dried cranberries</li>
  <li>1/2 cup peanut butter chips</li>
</ul>

<p>Mix any of these ingredients with the chocolate chips to create your own unique flavor.</p>

<h2>How to Make Nestle Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Nestle%20Chocolate%20Chip%20Cookies%20Recipe%20How%20To%20Make&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Nestle Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe How To Make" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that we have all the ingredients and tools, it's time to start baking. Follow these steps to make the perfect Nestle chocolate chip cookies:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).</li>
  <li>In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.</li>
  <li>In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened butter, white granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy and smooth.</li>
  <li>Add the eggs, one at a time, and beat well after each addition.</li>
  <li>Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, stirring until well combined.</li>
  <li>Stir in the Nestle Toll House Semi-Sweet Chocolate Morsels (and any additional ingredients you chose for the marinade).</li>
  <li>Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a non-stick baking mat.</li>
  <li>Drop the cookie dough by tablespoons onto the baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.</li>
  <li>Bake the cookies for 9-11 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.</li>
  <li>Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let the cookies cool on the sheet for 5 minutes.</li>
  <li>Transfer the cookies to a cooling rack and let them cool completely.</li>
</ol>

<p>Now that your cookies are ready, it's time to enjoy them. Here are some tips for cooking and serving:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Store cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days.</li>
  <li>You can freeze the cookie dough for up to 3 months. Just drop the cookie dough by tablespoons onto a baking sheet and freeze until solid. Then, transfer the frozen cookie dough balls to a freezer bag.</li>
  <li>To bake frozen cookie dough, just place the frozen dough balls on a lined baking sheet and bake for 12-14 minutes.</li>
  <li>You can also serve the cookies warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top.</li>
</ul>
