<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Bierock Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Bierock%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Bierock Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before we start making bierock, let's first gather all the ingredients we need:</p>

<ul>
<li>1 lb. ground beef</li>
<li>1 cup chopped onion</li>
<li>1 cup shredded cabbage</li>
<li>1 tsp salt</li>
<li>1 tsp black pepper</li>
<li>1 tsp garlic powder</li>
<li>1 tsp paprika</li>
<li>1 tsp Worcestershire sauce</li>
<li>1 lb. refrigerated bread dough</li>
<li>1 egg, beaten</li>
</ul>

<p>Make sure to prepare all the ingredients before starting with the recipe.</p>

<h2>For the Marinade Bierock Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Marinade%20Bierock&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Marinade Bierock" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>For the marinade, we will need:</p>

<ul>
<li>1/2 cup vinegar</li>
<li>1/2 cup water</li>
<li>1/4 cup sugar</li>
</ul>

<p>Combine all the marinade ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Set aside.</p>

<h2>How to Make Bierock Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Bierock&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Bierock" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that we have all the ingredients and kitchen tools ready, let's start making bierock:</p>

<ol>
<li>Preheat the oven to 375°F.</li>
<li>In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and shredded cabbage over medium heat until the beef is browned and the vegetables are tender.</li>
<li>Add the salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and Worcestershire sauce to the skillet. Stir well to combine.</li>
<li>Remove the skillet from the heat and set aside.</li>
<li>On a floured surface, roll out the refrigerated bread dough to a thickness of 1/4 inch.</li>
<li>Cut the dough into 6-inch squares.</li>
<li>Place a spoonful of the ground beef mixture onto each square.</li>
<li>Fold the dough over the mixture and pinch the edges together to seal.</li>
<li>Place the bierocks onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.</li>
<li>Brush each bierock with beaten egg.</li>
<li>Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the bierocks are golden brown.</li>
<li>Remove from the oven and brush each bierock with the marinade.</li>
<li>Serve hot and enjoy!</li>
</ol>

<ol>

<li>Brush each bierock with beaten egg.</li>

<li>Remove from the oven and brush each bierock with the marinade.</li>
<li>Serve hot and enjoy!</li>
</ol>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<center>

<p>Here are some frequently asked questions about bierock:</p>

<ol>
<li><strong>What is bierock?</strong> Bierock is a traditional German pastry that is made with ground beef, onion, and cabbage, enclosed in bread dough and baked until golden brown.</li>
<li><strong>What are the ingredients of bierock?</strong> The ingredients of bierock include ground beef, onion, cabbage, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, Worcestershire sauce, refrigerated bread dough, and egg.</li>
<li><strong>How do you make bierock?</strong> To make bierock, cook the ground beef, onion, and cabbage in a large skillet, add the seasonings, roll out the refrigerated bread dough, place a spoonful of the ground beef mixture onto each square, fold the dough over the mixture and pinch the edges together to seal, brush each bierock with beaten egg, and bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.</li>
<li><strong>What is the marinade for bierock?</strong> The marinade for bierock is made with vinegar, water, and sugar, and is brushed onto the bierocks after they are baked.</li>
<li><strong>Can bierock be frozen?</strong> Yes, bierock can be frozen. Once baked, let them cool completely and wrap them tightly in plastic wrap and aluminum foil. Label and date the package, and freeze for up to 3 months.</li>
</ol>
