Before we start with the recipe, let's take a look at the ingredients needed to make the best carrot cake recipe:

<ul>
<li>2 cups all-purpose flour</li>
<li>2 teaspoons baking powder</li>
<li>2 teaspoons baking soda</li>
<li>1 teaspoon salt</li>
<li>2 teaspoons ground cinnamon</li>
<li>4 large eggs</li>
<li>1 1/2 cups granulated sugar</li>
<li>1 1/2 cups vegetable oil</li>
<li>2 teaspoons vanilla extract</li>
<li>3 cups grated carrots</li>
<li>1 cup chopped walnuts</li>
<li>1/2 cup raisins</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade</h2>

To make the marinade for the carrot cake, we will need:

<ul>
<li>1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened</li>
<li>8 ounces cream cheese, softened</li>
<li>4 cups confectioner's sugar</li>
<li>1 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Best Carrot Cake Recipe</h2>

Now that we have all the ingredients and kitchen tools ready, let's start with the recipe:

<ol>
<li>Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Grease the cake pans with butter or cooking spray and line them with parchment paper.</li>
<li>In a mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Set aside.</li>
<li>In another mixing bowl, beat the eggs until fluffy. Add sugar, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well.</li>
<li>Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until well combined.</li>
<li>Add the grated carrots, chopped walnuts, and raisins. Mix until well combined.</li>
<li>Pour the batter into the prepared cake pans and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.</li>
<li>Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool on a cooling rack for 10 minutes. Then, remove them from the cake pans and let them cool completely.</li>
<li>To make the marinade, beat the softened butter and cream cheese until fluffy. Add the confectioner's sugar and vanilla extract. Mix well.</li>
<li>Place one cake layer on a cake stand. Spread the marinade over the cake layer. Place the second cake layer on top of the marinade and spread the remaining marinade over the cake.</li>
<li>Garnish the cake with chopped walnuts or raisins, if desired.</li>
<li>Serve and enjoy!</li>
</ol>
