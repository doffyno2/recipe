<p>Are you looking for a delicious and easy-to-make salad that will impress your guests? Look no further than the 7 layer salad! This classic dish is perfect for any occasion, from family dinners to potlucks and parties. In this article, we'll provide you with all the information you need to make a mouthwatering 7 layer salad that will have everyone asking for seconds.</p>

<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make 7 Layer Salad Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=7%20Layer%20Salad%20Ingredients&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="7 Layer Salad Ingredients" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before we get started, let's take a look at the ingredients you'll need to make a delicious 7 layer salad:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 head iceberg lettuce, chopped</li>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped green onions</li>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped celery</li>
  <li>1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper</li>
  <li>1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed</li>
  <li>1 cup mayonnaise</li>
  <li>1 cup sour cream</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons white sugar</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon garlic powder</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon onion powder</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon black pepper</li>
  <li>8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled</li>
  <li>1 cup shredded cheddar cheese</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade 7 Layer Salad Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=7%20Layer%20Salad%20Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="7 Layer Salad Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>The marinade is what gives the salad its unique flavor. Here's how to make it:</p>

<ul>
  <li>In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, white sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper.</li>
  <li>Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make 7 Layer Salad Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=7%20Layer%20Salad%20Steps&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="7 Layer Salad Steps" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and tools you need, let's get started on making the salad!</p>

<ol>
  <li>In a large salad bowl, layer the chopped iceberg lettuce, green onions, celery, green bell pepper, and thawed peas.</li>
  <li>Pour the marinade over the top of the vegetables, making sure to spread it evenly.</li>
  <li>Sprinkle the crumbled bacon and shredded cheddar cheese over the top of the salad.</li>
  <li>Cover the salad with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight.</li>
  <li>Before serving, toss the salad to ensure that all the ingredients are evenly coated with the marinade.</li>
</ol>
