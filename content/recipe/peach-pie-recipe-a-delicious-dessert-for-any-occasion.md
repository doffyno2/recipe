<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Peach Pie Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Peaches&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Peaches" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Before you start baking, make sure you have all the necessary ingredients on hand. Here's what you'll need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>6-7 ripe peaches, peeled and sliced</li>
  <li>1/2 cup granulated sugar</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons cornstarch</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon salt</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon lemon juice</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon unsalted butter, cut into small pieces</li>
  <li>1 double pie crust (store-bought or homemade)</li>
  <li>1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Peach Pie Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>The key to a delicious peach pie is the marinade. Here's how to make it:</p>

<ol>
  <li>In a large bowl, combine the sliced peaches, sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, salt, and lemon juice. Stir until the peaches are evenly coated.</li>
  <li>Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it sit for 30 minutes to an hour. This will allow the peaches to release their juices and create a thick, syrupy filling.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Make Peach Pie Recipe</h2>

<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Peach%20Pie&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Peach Pie" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Now that you have all your ingredients and kitchen tools ready, it's time to start baking! Follow these step-by-step instructions to make the perfect peach pie:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).</li>
  <li>Roll out your pie crust and place it in a 9-inch pie dish. Trim any excess dough around the edges.</li>
  <li>Pour the peach filling into the crust, making sure it's evenly distributed.</li>
  <li>Dot the top of the filling with small pieces of butter.</li>
  <li>Roll out your second pie crust and cut it into strips. Use the strips to create a lattice pattern on top of the filling.</li>
  <li>Brush the lattice crust with the beaten egg, making sure to cover all the exposed dough.</li>
  <li>Bake the pie for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.</li>
  <li>Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for at least 30 minutes before serving.</li>
</ol>
