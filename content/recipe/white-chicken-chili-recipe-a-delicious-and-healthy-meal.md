<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make White Chicken Chili Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20White%20Chicken%20Chili%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make White Chicken Chili Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>To make this delicious White Chicken Chili, you will need the following ingredients:</p>
<ul>
  <li>1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces</li>
  <li>1 onion, chopped</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic, minced</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon olive oil</li>
  <li>2 cans of white beans, drained and rinsed</li>
  <li>1 can of diced green chilies</li>
  <li>1 can of corn, drained</li>
  <li>4 cups of chicken broth</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon ground cumin</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon chili powder</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon salt</li>
  <li>1/4 teaspoon black pepper</li>
  <li>1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make White Chicken Chili Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20White%20Chicken%20Chili%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make White Chicken Chili Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

<p>Follow these simple steps to make this delicious White Chicken Chili:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.</li>
  <li>Add the chicken and sauté for 5-7 minutes until cooked through.</li>
  <li>Add the onion and garlic and sauté for an additional 2-3 minutes until softened.</li>
  <li>Add the white beans, green chilies, corn, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.</li>
  <li>Bring the chili to a simmer and cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.</li>
  <li>Remove from heat and stir in the chopped cilantro.</li>
  <li>Serve hot and enjoy!</li>
</ol>
