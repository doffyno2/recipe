<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Cheesy Potatoes Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20Needed%20To%20Make%20Cheesy%20Potatoes%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients Needed To Make Cheesy Potatoes Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>To make our cheesy potatoes recipe, you'll need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
    <li>6-8 medium-sized potatoes</li>
    <li>1 cup shredded cheddar cheese</li>
    <li>1/2 cup sour cream</li>
    <li>1/4 cup butter</li>
    <li>2 cloves garlic, minced</li>
    <li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
</ul>

    <li>1 tablespoon dried rosemary</li>
    <li>1 tablespoon dried oregano</li>

</ul>

<h2>How to Make Cheesy Potatoes Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Cheesy%20Potatoes%20Recipe&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Cheesy Potatoes Recipe" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>
<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and tools ready, let's start making some delicious cheesy potatoes:</p>

<ol>
    <li>Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).</li>
    <li>Peel and slice the potatoes into thin rounds.</li>
    <li>In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced potatoes, melted butter, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Toss until the potatoes are evenly coated.</li>
    <li>If you want to add some extra flavor, prepare the marinade by mixing all the ingredients in a separate bowl. Pour the marinade over the potatoes and toss until they are evenly coated.</li>
    <li>Transfer the potatoes to a greased baking dish and spread them out in an even layer.</li>
    <li>Bake the potatoes for 25-30 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly crispy.</li>
    <li>Remove the baking dish from the oven and sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the potatoes.</li>
    <li>Return the baking dish to the oven and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.</li>
    <li>Remove the baking dish from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.</li>
    <li>Top with sour cream and garnish with fresh herbs if desired.</li>
</ol>
