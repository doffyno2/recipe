<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Ratatouille Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ingredients%20For%20Ratatouille&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ingredients For Ratatouille" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

Ratatouille is a classic French dish that is made from fresh vegetables. The ingredients needed to make ratatouille are simple and easy to find. Here is a list of ingredients you will need to make ratatouille:

<ul>
    <li>1 large eggplant, cut into bite-size pieces</li>
    <li>2 large zucchinis, cut into bite-size pieces</li>
    <li>1 large red bell pepper, cut into bite-size pieces</li>
    <li>1 large yellow onion, chopped</li>
    <li>4 cloves garlic, minced</li>
    <li>1 can diced tomatoes, undrained</li>
    <li>1/4 cup olive oil</li>
    <li>1 tsp dried basil</li>
    <li>1 tsp dried oregano</li>
    <li>1 tsp dried thyme</li>
    <li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=Ratatouille%20Marinade&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="Ratatouille Marinade" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

To make the marinade for the ratatouille, you will need the following ingredients:

<ul>
    <li>1/4 cup olive oil</li>
    <li>3 tbsp red wine vinegar</li>
    <li>1 tsp sugar</li>
    <li>1 tsp salt</li>
    <li>1/2 tsp black pepper</li>
</ul>

    <li>A wooden spoon</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Make Ratatouille Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Make%20Ratatouille&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Make Ratatouille" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

Now that you have all the ingredients and tools ready, let's start making ratatouille:

<ol>
    <li>In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, sugar, salt, and black pepper to make the marinade. Set aside.</li>
    <li>In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat.</li>
    <li>Add the chopped onions and minced garlic to the skillet and sauté for 5 minutes or until the onions are translucent.</li>
    <li>Add the diced eggplant, zucchini, and red bell pepper to the skillet and sauté for 10 minutes or until the vegetables are slightly tender.</li>
    <li>Add the canned tomatoes and the marinade to the skillet and stir to combine.</li>
    <li>Reduce the heat to low and let the ratatouille simmer for 20-30 minutes or until the vegetables are fully cooked.</li>
    <li>Season with salt and pepper to taste.</li>
    <li>Serve hot with crusty bread or over rice.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Ratatouille Recipe</h2>
<center>
	<img src="https://tse1.mm.bing.net/th?q=How%20To%20Cook%20Ratatouille&w=800&h=500&c=1&rs=1" alt="How To Cook Ratatouille" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
</center>

Ratatouille is best cooked slowly over low heat to allow the flavors to meld together. Here are some tips on how to cook ratatouille:

<ul>
    <li>Cut the vegetables into similar sizes to ensure even cooking.</li>
    <li>Sauté the vegetables before adding the tomatoes to bring out their flavor.</li>
    <li>Let the ratatouille simmer over low heat for at least 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.</li>
    <li>Season with salt and pepper to taste.</li>
</ul>
