<h2>Ingredients Needed to Make Sloppy Joe Recipe</h2>

<p>If you are looking for a quick and easy meal that is loved by everyone, then you must try the classic sloppy joe recipe. This recipe requires very few ingredients that you can easily find in your pantry. Here are the ingredients that you will need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 pound ground beef</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon olive oil</li>
  <li>1 onion, diced</li>
  <li>1 green pepper, diced</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon garlic powder</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon paprika</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon chili powder</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon salt</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon black pepper</li>
  <li>1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce</li>
  <li>1/4 cup ketchup</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons brown sugar</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar</li>
  <li>8 hamburger buns</li>
</ul>

<h2>For the Marinade Sloppy Joe Recipe</h2>

<p>The key to making the best sloppy joes is the marinade. It is important to let the meat simmer in the marinade for at least 10 minutes. Here is the marinade recipe:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1/4 cup ketchup</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons brown sugar</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar</li>
</ul>

<p>Before you start cooking, make sure you have the following kitchen tools:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Large skillet or frying pan</li>

<h2>How to Make Sloppy Joe Recipe</h2>

<p>Now that you have all the ingredients and kitchen tools, let's start cooking the best sloppy joes at home:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat.</li>
  <li>Add the ground beef and cook until browned, stirring occasionally.</li>
  <li>Add the onion and green pepper and cook until softened, stirring occasionally.</li>
  <li>Add the garlic powder, paprika, chili powder, salt, and black pepper and stir to combine.</li>
  <li>Add the tomato sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and apple cider vinegar and stir to combine.</li>
  <li>Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.</li>
  <li>To assemble the sloppy joes, place a scoop of the meat mixture on a hamburger bun and serve.</li>
</ol>

<h2>How to Cook Sloppy Joe Recipe</h2>

<p>The best way to cook the sloppy joe recipe is to use a large skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat. Make sure to brown the ground beef first and then add the onions and green peppers. It is important to let the meat simmer in the marinade for at least 10 minutes to get the best flavor. Once the meat is fully cooked and the marinade has been added, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for at least 10 minutes.</p>
